## Baguio Craft Brewery
*KM 4, Ben Palispis Highway · Brewery*
Very good craft beer, solid food, usually excellent music — a bit pricey by local standards but not unreasonably so.

## The Original Good Taste Cafe & Restaurant
*Lapu Lapu St, Baguio City · Filipino*
Extremely popular for its enormous portions and incredibly low prices — simple hearty Filipino comfort food done right.

## UMMASON Restaurant
*Baguio City · Korean BBQ*
A basic cash-only Korean BBQ place with excellent value — unlimited meat and side dishes for under 400 Php per person.
