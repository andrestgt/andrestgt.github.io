Maybe I'm wrong, but it feels like this place is struggling. Craft beer doesn't seem to have a big market in Puerto, and when we visited on a Saturday night around 10 pm, the bar was completely empty. Even more ironic, given the name of the place, there were no beers on tap available. They do have a good selection of craft beers in cans and bottles, but an empty bar just doesn't have much atmosphere. It's a real pity as this place has so much potential.
