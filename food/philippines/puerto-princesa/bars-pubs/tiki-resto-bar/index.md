I know I would have loved this place twenty years ago. The live band is actually pretty good, but they don't play much — it's mostly a DJ blasting less enjoyable music. Most beers were out of stock, though the ones available were reasonably priced. There's a 50 PHP entrance fee, which would be worth it if the band had more stage time. That said, it's a casual, good-natured, anything-goes kind of place with a fun mix of locals, Western backpackers, and older expats.
