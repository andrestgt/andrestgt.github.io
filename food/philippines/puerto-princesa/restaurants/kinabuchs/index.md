KGB, as everyone calls it, is still exactly as I remembered it from 2009, a great open-air grill and one of Puerto's institutions. Good food, huge portions, and while it's obviously pricier now, it's still reasonable for what you get. The grilled tuna belly in particular was delicious. The perfect place to end a long day, or a long trip, over cold beer and something off the grill.
