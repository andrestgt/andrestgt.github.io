This place should be great, but my experience here was really bad. First, they had run out of rucola when I tried to order a pizza requiring this ingredient. Then my cappuccino arrived in a plastic cup with cinnamon on top — with a name like La Bella, you would expect at least a small amount of Italian café culture. They were willing to replace my cappuccino, and the proper one I got was good.
