# Bars & Pubs

---

## El Chupacabra
*Poblacion, Makati · Bar & Grill*
One of the best hangouts in Makati — inexpensive booze, genuinely tasty Tex-Fil-Mex food, and always lively.
→ [Read the full review](/food/philippines/manila/bars-pubs/el-chupacabra/)

---

## Murray D'Vine
*BGC, Taguig · Bar*
Probably the best bar around High Street for happy hour — ice-cold beers, good drinks specials, and delicious sisig.
→ [Read the full review](/food/philippines/manila/bars-pubs/murray-dvine/)

---

## RUE Forbes Town
*Forbespark, BGC · Bar*
Loud and chaotic but good-natured — drinks are expensive but the nachos supreme are particularly good.
→ [Read the full review](/food/philippines/manila/bars-pubs/rue-forbes-town/)

---

## Erras Bar
*Adriatico St, Malate · Bar*
Long-running Malate institution — never closes, cheap beers, always lively, and surprisingly decent food.
→ [Read the full review](/food/philippines/manila/bars-pubs/erras-bar/)

---

## Oarhouse Pub
*Malate, Manila · Pub*
One of the few remnants of old Malate — cozy pub with good grub, affordable drinks, and craft beers now available.
→ [Read the full review](/food/philippines/manila/bars-pubs/oarhouse-pub/)

---

## Cafe Cubana
*P Burgos St, Makati · Bar*
An absolute rip-off — high prices for artificially tasting cocktails with old mint leaves; no reason to come here.
→ [Read the full review](/food/philippines/manila/bars-pubs/cafe-cubana/)
