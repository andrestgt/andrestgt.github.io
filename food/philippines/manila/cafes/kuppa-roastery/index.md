Kuppa uses good espresso beans and the coffee preparation itself is perfectly competent, but the milk tasted unpleasantly off and ruined the overall cup. Which is a shame, because the lamb caldereta was excellent and very good value by BGC standards. Unfortunately I came for the coffee, and that part disappointed.
