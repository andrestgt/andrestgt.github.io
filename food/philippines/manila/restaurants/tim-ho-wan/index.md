Tim Ho Wan still serves excellent dim sum and remains well worth visiting despite being more expensive than the original one in Hong Kong. Expect around ₱600 per person for a fairly substantial meal. Personally, I slightly prefer the Hong Kong version of their famous pork buns, which seem sweeter here.
