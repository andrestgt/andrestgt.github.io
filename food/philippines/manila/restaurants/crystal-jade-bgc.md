Bonifacio High Street, 7th Ave, Taguig, Metro Manila, Philippines

The main reason to visit any Crystal Jade branch is usually the dim sum, and this one is no exception. The BGC branch is more polished and upscale than most, with prices to match. The dim sum selection is not huge, but quality is consistently very good. At around 200 Php a basket it feels expensive, but proper dim sum options in Manila are surprisingly limited.
