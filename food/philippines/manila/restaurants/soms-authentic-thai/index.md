Despite the name, this is very much Thai food adjusted for Filipino tastes. The tom yum soup was oddly sweet, the noodle soup lacked herbs and punch, and while the som tam was acceptable enough, it felt heavily toned down and sanitised. Not somewhere I would revisit.
