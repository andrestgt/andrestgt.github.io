This rustic beach bar is the cheapest spot with a prime seaside location in El Nido. Strangely, you won't find many backpackers here — they seem to prefer the pricier, posher places with louder music. Personally, I find this to be the most pleasant beachfront bar in town. It has vibes of the old El Nido from the 00s before mass tourism made it almost unrecognisable. The only drawback is that they don't serve any food with the drinks, not even basic pulutan.
