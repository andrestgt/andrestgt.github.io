It's definitely not the friendliest place in the Philippines, but the location is fantastic. This is a proper beach restaurant, and if they can grill you a whole fresh fish, definitely go for it. The prices for the grilled fish are very fair — we paid 550 pesos for a jackfish that was easily enough for two. I wasn't completely convinced by some of their other dishes, which didn't seem to offer great value, but for fresh fish, this place is hard to beat.
