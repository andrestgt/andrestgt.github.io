# Restaurants

---

## Leonida's
*Taytay, Palawan · Filipino*
An unexpected gem in the middle of nowhere — beautifully decorated garden café with outstanding Filipino food and fair prices.
→ [Read the full review](/food/philippines/el-nido/restaurants/leonidas/)

---

## Socorro Hills View
*Route 490, El Nido · Filipino*
The nicest pit stop along Route 490 — sweeping sea views, tasty shakes, and solid food at moderate prices.
→ [Read the full review](/food/philippines/el-nido/restaurants/socorro-hills-view/)

---

## El Nido Boutique Artcafe
*El Nido Town · International*
No longer the focal point for travellers but still gets things right — solid food, nicer décor, and a pleasant second-floor terrace.
→ [Read the full review](/food/philippines/el-nido/restaurants/el-nido-boutique-artcafe/)

---

## Patron Mexican Restobar
*El Nido Town · Mexican*
Extremely popular for its cheerful atmosphere and cheap long drinks — solid Mexican food and reasonable prices by El Nido standards.
→ [Read the full review](/food/philippines/el-nido/restaurants/patron-mexican-restobar/)

---

## Pong's Pares El Nido
*El Nido Town · Filipino*
Pungent, heavy, oily, delicious — the pares mami and tofu sisig are excellent, best enjoyed as a hangover cure.
→ [Read the full review](/food/philippines/el-nido/restaurants/pongs-pares/)

---

## Carinderia by the Bay
*El Nido Town · Filipino*
Not the friendliest place in the Philippines, but the location is fantastic and the fresh grilled fish is hard to beat.
→ [Read the full review](/food/philippines/el-nido/restaurants/carinderia-by-the-bay/)

**Price level note:**
