How unexpected to find such a beautiful garden café, seemingly in the middle of nowhere, somewhere in provincial Palawan. Leonida's is truly a gem. Not only is it beautifully decorated, but the service is also very friendly, and the food is on par with some of the better Filipino restaurants in the big cities. Their crispy pork kare-kare was absolutely mouth-wateringly delicious. Prices are very reasonable for this kind of food quality in such an environment. They even serve really good, proper coffees, though they're a bit on the pricey side.
