There may be more beautiful beaches in greater El Nido than Bucana, but for pure beach restaurant atmosphere, Chill'n Sea is hard to beat. Sitting on the sand with cheap cocktails and seafood sizzlers while watching a gorgeous sunset doesn't get much better than this.

The food itself is absolutely fine by local standards, and prices are fair considering the million-dollar location.
