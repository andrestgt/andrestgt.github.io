Back in the day, when Nacpan was still possibly the most beautiful beach in the world, there were no beach restaurants here and hardly any development at all. Times have changed, and Nacpan is much busier these days, though it remains a stunning place.

This friendly beachfront spot is a great place to take a break from the sun. You can enjoy a cold beer, a fresh coconut, or a hearty serving of sinuglaw while taking in the scenery. It is also a convenient place to leave your belongings while you go for a swim.

Prices are still reasonable, and of all the places along the beach, this was the one that appealed to me the most. Very recommendable.
