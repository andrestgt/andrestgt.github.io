A very quiet resort on a hill above Taytay, with beautiful views over the town and bay from the restaurant terrace. The service is slow and the food is so-so, but as a rest stop on the long ride between El Nido and Puerto Princesa it's a lovely place to pause. With more time, I'd happily have broken the trip here for a night.
