# Cafés

Covers Panglao island and Tagbilaran city.

---

## Gavroche
*Panglao · Bakery, Café*
An excellent little boulangerie with very good croissants and proper bread — the coffee is a bit disappointing, but the pastries make up for it.
→ [Read the full review](/food/philippines/bohol-panglao/cafes/gavroche/)

---

## Nikita's Coffee Shop
*Alona Beach, Panglao · Café*
Looks like a greasy spoon but oddly serves some of the best espresso drinks around Alona — a proper flat white for around 95 Php.
→ [Read the full review](/food/philippines/bohol-panglao/cafes/nikitas-coffee-shop/)

---

## The Buzzz Café – Island City Mall
*Tagbilaran City · Café, Filipino*
Surprisingly good bistro food for Tagbilaran, with prices in the 200–300 Php range that the quality largely justifies.
→ [Read the full review](/food/philippines/bohol-panglao/cafes/the-buzzz-cafe-island-city-mall/)

---

## Estrella Bakery
*Tagbilaran City · Bakery, Café*
Tiny bakery serving probably the best cappuccino on the island using proper Illy beans for just 80 Php — no wifi though.
→ [Read the full review](/food/philippines/bohol-panglao/cafes/estrella-bakery/)

---

## Tamper Coffee & Brunch
*Tagbilaran City · Café*
Good atmosphere and competent baristas let down by mediocre beans — a real missed opportunity.
→ [Read the full review](/food/philippines/bohol-panglao/cafes/tamper-coffee/)

---

## Bo's Coffee – Island City Mall
*Tagbilaran City · Café*
Noticeably worse than most Bo's branches — overly hot, too milky, and very average for the price.
→ [Read the full review](/food/philippines/bohol-panglao/cafes/bos-coffee-island-city-mall/)

---

## Brewpoint Coffee Club
*Tagbilaran City · Café*
The cappuccino arrived weak, overheated, and without properly frothed milk — unforgivable for a place calling itself a coffee club.
