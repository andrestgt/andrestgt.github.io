Brewpoint could be a nice café, but the coffee is genuinely poor. The cappuccino arrived weak, overheated, and without properly frothed milk, which is fairly unforgivable for a place calling itself a coffee club. The food is acceptable enough, but coffee clearly isn't the strong point here.
