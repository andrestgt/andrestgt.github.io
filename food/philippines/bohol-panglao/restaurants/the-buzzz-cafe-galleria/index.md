The Buzzz Café mostly caters to the local palate, so many dishes lean heavily towards the sweet side. Still, they deserve credit for offering fresh salads and vegetables, which remain surprisingly rare in much of the Philippines. The farm branch is more worthwhile overall, if only for the views.
