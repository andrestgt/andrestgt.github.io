F. Torres St, Poblacion District, Davao City, Davao del Sur, Philippines

The atmosphere is cramped and cafeteria-like, but there is a very good reason to come here: the Spanish-Filipino food is quite delicious. The calamares rellenos en su tinta, callos tripe stew, and braised tongue lengua estofado were all excellent. Most dishes are under 300 Php with generous portions. Worth calling ahead about corkage if you want to bring your own wine.
