Frankly, this is a terrible restaurant physically — cramped and designed like a cafeteria. However, the Spanish-Filipino food they serve is quite delicious. The calamares rellenos en su tinta, the callos tripe stew and the braised tongue lengua estofado were all excellent. Most dishes are under 300 Php, portions are big and the food is really tasty.
