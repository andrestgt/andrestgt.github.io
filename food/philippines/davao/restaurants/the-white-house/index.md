The White House is a beautiful restaurant in a heritage building with a show kitchen and all the trappings of a fine dining establishment. The atmosphere is excellent, but for everything else — from service to food — there's still room for improvement. The young chef is talented and bold, even if not all dishes worked well. I really enjoyed the salmon sashimi, the glazed pork belly and the Japanese cheesecake. Prices for food and wine are reasonable for such a fancy restaurant, however service and consistency could and should be improved.
