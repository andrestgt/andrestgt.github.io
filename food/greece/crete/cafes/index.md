# Cafés in Crete


## BOUGATSA IORDANIS
Chania · Café
Excellent place for breakfast or a quick snack. A plate costs around 3€, is surprisingly filling, and the combination of cheese filling and perfectly made dough is hard to beat.
→ [Read the full review](./bougatsa-iordanis/)

## COFFEE ISLAND
Hersonissos · Café
Serves decent coffee at very fair prices — cappuccino only around 1,60€. Mainly designed as a takeaway café, so don't expect proper mugs or comfortable seating.
→ [Read the full review](./coffee-island/)

## HUB CAFE+LOUNGE
Iraklion · Café
Relaxed Old Town lounge with very good coffee. Prices are high by Cretan standards, but the cappuccino was the best anywhere on the island.
→ [Read the full review](./hub-cafe-lounge/)

## KOSTA KOSTA CAFE
Malia · Café
Modern-looking café with comfortable sofas and outdoor seating. Cappuccino costs around 2,50€ — slightly expensive for the area, though the quality is decent enough.
→ [Read the full review](./kosta-kosta-cafe/)

## ΖΕΜΠΊΛΗΣ
Rethymno · Café
Mostly a takeaway coffee place near the bus station. All coffees cost only 1€ — very convenient if you're waiting around.
