# Restaurants in Crete


## ΑΘΙΒΟΛΕΣ ΤΟΥ ΚΑΡΑ
Kalamaki · Taverna
Very friendly family-run taverna with excellent food at genuinely fair prices — most dishes around 5–7€. The escargots and spicy baked feta are particularly good.
→ [Read the full review](./ativoles-tou-kara/)

## BLUE HOUSE
Loutro · Restaurant
A highly recommended restaurant in a wonderful setting. The food is simple, honest, and home cooked — the octopus with spinach and the snails are especially good.
→ [Read the full review](./blue-house/)

## KOUZOUMAMA
Kissamos · Restaurant
One of the best places to eat in Kissamos. Fresh, very good food with generous portions and reasonable prices. Dessert and generous glasses of raki on the house.
→ [Read the full review](./kouzoumama/)

## KOUKOUVAGIA – CRETAN CUISINE
Iraklion · Restaurant
Looks slightly touristy but felt very local when we visited. Proper hearty Cretan cooking with cheap wine. Delivers exactly the kind of traditional experience many visitors are looking for.
→ [Read the full review](./koukouvagia/)

## PANORAMA CAFE – TAVERNA
Kefali · Taverna
Small village taverna with gorgeous mountain views and very friendly service. The moussaka was probably the best in Crete — rich, creamy, and generously filled.
→ [Read the full review](./panorama-cafe-taverna/)

## PLATANOS
Crete · Taverna
During the day a lovely shaded village taverna for a relaxed lunch or a glass of wine. Prices are fair and the atmosphere is very pleasant.
→ [Read the full review](./platanos/)

## ΒΑΣΙΛΙΚΉ ΚΟΥΖΊΝΑ
Rethymno · Restaurant
Despite the prime tourist location, serves honest Mediterranean food at very reasonable prices. Even the inexpensive house wine is surprisingly good.
→ [Read the full review](./vasiliki-kouzina/)

## ΑΓΑΠΉΝΗΣ ΟΙΝΟΜΑΓΕΙΡΕΊΟ
Chania · Restaurant
Tiny working-class eatery inside the market serving wonderfully traditional Cretan food in huge portions. Excellent value.
→ [Read the full review](./agapinis-oinomageireio/)

## XATIRI
Iraklion · Restaurant
Excellent location and a very pleasant place either for a full meal or simply some wine with small snacks alongside.
→ [Read the full review](./xatiri/)

**Price level note:**
