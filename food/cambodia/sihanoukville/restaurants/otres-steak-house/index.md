This place feels slightly more refined than most restaurants around Otres Village, with proper napkins, a wine fridge, and a generally more polished atmosphere. The Khmer food is genuinely good — seafood especially — and mains remain reasonably priced at around 5$. Charging 2$ for canned juice is pushing it a bit, though.
