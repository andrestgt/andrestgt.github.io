If you're seriously hungry, give this one a try. This is a simple outdoor place by the river a bit south of town with a laid-back beer garden feel and a varied menu of Central European and Thai dishes. Be warned — the portions are huge. The schnitzel here is more like three schnitzels, thin, tender and juicy, best eaten with some tasty potato salad. The cheese spätzle are as good as any you'll find in Southern Germany. Perhaps the biggest surprise are the authentic Thai curries, good for 2–3 people sharing at around $5.
