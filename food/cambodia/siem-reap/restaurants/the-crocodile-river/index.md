If you're seriously hungry, give this one a try. This is a simple outdoor place by the river with a laid-back beer garden feel and a varied menu of Central European and Thai dishes. The portions are enormous — the schnitzel is more like three schnitzels, thin, tender and juicy. The cheese spätzle are as good as any you'd find in Southern Germany. The Thai curries cost around 5$ but come in Central European portions and are good for 2–3 people sharing.
