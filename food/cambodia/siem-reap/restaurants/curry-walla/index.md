Curry Walla used to be my favourite Indian restaurant in Siem Reap, but they plain and simple cheated me: the Chicken Tikka Masala was just a regular cheap chicken curry (tikka should be marinated, boneless and tender), and the supposedly lamb korma appeared to contain cheap beef at a premium lamb price. I can taste the difference between lamb and beef. I've since found a new Indian place and will never go back to Curry Walla.
