Bella's is unapologetically American in style, with very large portions and comfort food aimed squarely at travellers craving a break from Khmer cuisine. The burger was decent, although expensive for Siem Reap, but the fries were disappointingly soggy. Feels like a place that could be considerably better with a bit more care.
