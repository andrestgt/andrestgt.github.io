Da Roberto is located in a lovely little grove just outside Siem Reap and definitely worth the short trip out of town. The pizza is among the best in Siem Reap, with genuinely good cheeses and very well-made toppings, and the carpaccio is excellent as well. Prices are perfectly reasonable for the quality.
