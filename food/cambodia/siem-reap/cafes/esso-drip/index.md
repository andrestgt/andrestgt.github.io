Esso Drip is a small, friendly coffee shop serving one of the best cappuccinos in Siem Reap. Prices are high by Cambodian standards, but the quality justifies it. They also used to give a proper French croissant with each coffee, although I'm not sure whether that was only a temporary promotion.
