# Cafés

---

## Ellipse Coffee
*Kampot · Café*
A very welcome addition to Kampot's café scene — air conditioning, genuinely comfortable seating, and a surprisingly good cappuccino for only 1.75$.
→ [Read the full review](/food/cambodia/kampot-kep/cafes/ellipse-coffee/)

---

## Barista Coffee
*Durian Roundabout, Kampot · Café*
Modern coffee shop with an impressive espresso machine — nicely prepared cappuccino with fresh milk for around 7k.
→ [Read the full review](/food/cambodia/kampot-kep/cafes/barista-coffee/)

---

## Café Malay – The Garden Café
*Kampot · Café*
Good coffees, nice Khmer and German food in big portions, and the best cakes in town — German quality baking.
→ [Read the full review](/food/cambodia/kampot-kep/cafes/cafe-malay/)

---

## L'Epi d'Or
*Kampot · Bakery, Café*
Pleasant French-style bakery with attractive décor and a comfortable couch — coffees are expensive by Kampot standards and the wifi is painfully slow.
→ [Read the full review](/food/cambodia/kampot-kep/cafes/lepi-dor/)

---

## Cafe Espresso Roastery
*Kampot · Café*
Once the best coffee in provincial Cambodia, now undermined by a switch to homogenised milk without informing customers.
→ [Read the full review](/food/cambodia/kampot-kep/cafes/cafe-espresso-roastery/)

---

## Saravoan Kep
*Kep · Café, Restaurant*
Beautiful sea-view terrace with proper Lavazza beans let down badly by poor coffee preparation — better staff training could make this excellent.
→ [Read the full review](/food/cambodia/kampot-kep/cafes/saravoan-kep/)
