Finding a place like Divino is every travelling foodie's dream — imagine stumbling over a super casual hole-in-the-wall Italian place in a sleepy colonial Old Town in the Cambodian province. Divino is the real deal: simple Italian home cooking at its best. They make their own pasta, bake their own delicious bread, every dish is fresh and made from scratch. They also have some excellent Italian wines at bargain prices. We had carpaccio, ravioli and gnocchi, and they were all delicious. When you're in Kampot and you like food, don't give Divino a miss.
