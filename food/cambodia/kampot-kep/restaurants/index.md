# Restaurants

---

## Divino Italian Restaurant
*Fish Island Rd, Kampot · Italian*
The real deal — simple Italian home cooking with fresh homemade pasta, own-baked bread, and excellent wines at bargain prices.
→ [Read the full review](/food/cambodia/kampot-kep/restaurants/divino-italian-restaurant/)

---

## Rikitikitavi
*Riverside Rd, Kampot · Khmer Fusion*
Upscale riverside dining with fantastic terrace views, good Khmer fusion food, and generous mains.
→ [Read the full review](/food/cambodia/kampot-kep/restaurants/rikitikitavi/)

---

## Veronica's Garden
*Street 735, Kampot · Khmer*
Good place at the refurbished Old Market — decent Khmer food with nice touches and good value wines.
→ [Read the full review](/food/cambodia/kampot-kep/restaurants/veronicas-garden/)

---

## Ecstatic Restaurant
*Old Market, Kampot · Khmer, International*
More about huge shakes and the great Old Market location than the pizza — tasty Khmer food and very good value.
→ [Read the full review](/food/cambodia/kampot-kep/restaurants/ecstatic-restaurant/)

---

## Kampot Old Bridge Kitchen
*Kampot · Khmer*
Cheap and very good Khmer food — the Kampot pepper squid is delicious; skip the warm wine.
→ [Read the full review](/food/cambodia/kampot-kep/restaurants/kampot-old-bridge-kitchen/)

---

## Ecran Noodles
*Street 735, Kampot · Chinese, Asian*
Great place for homemade dumplings and noodles — a solid budget meal or quick fill.
→ [Read the full review](/food/cambodia/kampot-kep/restaurants/ecran-noodles/)

## Lemongrass Bistro
*Kampot · Thai, Khmer*
Quirky Thai-Khmer spot that comes highly recommended, but I found the food surprisingly bland — at least prices are low.
