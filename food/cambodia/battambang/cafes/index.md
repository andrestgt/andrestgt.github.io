## Kinyei Cafe
*Street 1.5, Battambang · Café*
Very simple and not especially comfortable, but they make probably the best cappuccino in Battambang — reason enough to come.

## Cafe Eden
*Riverside Rd, Battambang · Café*
A long-standing favourite along the river road with decent, moderately priced bar food, improved coffee, sofas, and good wifi.
