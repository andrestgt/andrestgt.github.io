The Kitchen looks inviting enough from the outside, especially in Battambang's attractive Old Town, but the reality was deeply disappointing. The sight of tour buses parked outside should probably have been warning enough. The coffee was genuinely awful, bitter, watery, and served so absurdly hot it burnt my lips. Sending it back achieved almost nothing. The food was equally underwhelming, with an allegedly refined fish dish turning out to be little more than mediocre battered fish with rice at inflated tourist-trap prices. Battambang has plenty of far better cafés and restaurants nowadays.
