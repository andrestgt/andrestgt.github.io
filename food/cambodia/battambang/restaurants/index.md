# Restaurants

---

## Jaan Bai Restaurant
*Street 2, Battambang · Pan-Asian*
The best meal in town, contemporary pan-Asian food with outstanding ribs and the best cappuccino in Battambang.
→ [Read the full review](/food/cambodia/battambang/restaurants/jaan-bai-restaurant/)

---

## Delux Villa Restaurant
*Battambang · International*
Worth visiting even if you're not staying at the hotel; the fried wontons and squid with Kampot pepper are particularly good.
→ [Read the full review](/food/cambodia/battambang/restaurants/delux-villa-restaurant/)

---

## Khmer Delight Restaurant
*Street 119, Battambang · Khmer*
Pleasant tourist restaurant with a surprisingly decent wine list, standard Khmer fare, tasty but not especially memorable.
→ [Read the full review](/food/cambodia/battambang/restaurants/khmer-delight-restaurant/)

---

## Au Cabaret Vert
*Battambang · Khmer, French*
Good food but rather too expensive for Battambang, better options exist unless you're specifically craving foie gras.
