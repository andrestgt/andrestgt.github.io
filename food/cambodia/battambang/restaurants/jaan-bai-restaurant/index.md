The modern, air-conditioned Jaan Bai has been a welcome addition to Battambang's culinary scene. The food is contemporary pan-Asian and, while the menu is small, there's enough interesting dishes to warrant subsequent visits. The ribs are the most expensive item on the menu but absolutely delicious and worth the visit alone. Finally, I was pleasantly surprised by their cappuccino, which is the best in town, expertly prepared. Jaan Bai serves tasty and classy food at reasonable prices. Not all dishes are perfect, but it's most likely the best meal in town.
