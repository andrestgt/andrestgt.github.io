Hard to define — club, live music venue, tapas bar, café, restaurant: it really depends on time and the day you visit. I experienced very good service. The set lunch menu at 10$ for 3 courses with coffee was a real bargain — presentation and quality left nothing to be desired, it was obvious the chef knows his métier. Drinks are reasonably priced for this kind of place and their mojito was great. I will return for more.
