## Dim Sum Emperors
*Central Market · Chinese, Dim Sum*
My favourite dim sum in Phnom Penh — large selection, reasonable prices, served from early morning until late.
→ [Read the full review](/food/cambodia/phnom-penh/restaurants/dim-sum-emperors/)

---

## Dim Sum Emperors TK Avenue
*TK Avenue Mall · Chinese, Dim Sum*
Same quality as the Central Market branch but much calmer — probably the best value dim sum in Phnom Penh.
→ [Read the full review](/food/cambodia/phnom-penh/restaurants/dim-sum-emperors-tk/)

---

## Mok Mony Restaurant
*Street 294 · Khmer*
Carefully prepared and authentic Khmer dishes — the beef salad stood out for the excellent quality of the meat.
→ [Read the full review](/food/cambodia/phnom-penh/restaurants/mok-mony-restaurant/)

---

## Flavors of India
*Street 63 · Indian*
The best of the Indian restaurants I've tried in Phnom Penh — the korma is very good and they use decent cuts of meat.
→ [Read the full review](/food/cambodia/phnom-penh/restaurants/flavors-of-india/)

---

## Eleven One Kitchen
*Toul Tom Poung · Khmer*
Welcoming neighbourhood restaurant with low prices, good Khmer food, and cheap cold beer from the tap.
→ [Read the full review](/food/cambodia/phnom-penh/restaurants/eleven-one-kitchen/)

---

## Open Wine
*Phnom Penh · French*
Good French food with great lunch deals — shame the wine list has become overpriced.
→ [Read the full review](/food/cambodia/phnom-penh/restaurants/open-wine/)

---

## The Sushi Bar
*Phnom Penh · Japanese*
Fresh sashimi and decent rolls at relatively affordable prices — still probably my favourite sushi place in the city.
→ [Read the full review](/food/cambodia/phnom-penh/restaurants/the-sushi-bar/)

---

## Mount Everest
*Street 294 · Indian*
One of the better Indian options in Phnom Penh — good, generous curries at 5–7$ per main, though no alcohol served.
→ [Read the full review](/food/cambodia/phnom-penh/restaurants/mount-everest/)

---

## Nimol
*Preah Ang Yukanthor St · Khmer*
Local outdoor eatery with a good English menu of Khmer classics — raw beef salad and fish amok at around 5$ per dish.
→ [Read the full review](/food/cambodia/phnom-penh/restaurants/nimol/)

---

## Pink Elephant Happy Pizza
*Sisowath Quay · Pizza, Khmer*
Cheap, cheerful, and exactly what it says — decent Asian dishes and the riverside location is hard to beat.
→ [Read the full review](/food/cambodia/phnom-penh/restaurants/pink-elephant-happy-pizza/)

## Comme à la Maison

Excellent baguettes from the deli, but the restaurant is hit and miss — the salade chèvre chaud was great, the boudin noir disappointing.

## Friends the Restaurant
*Street 13 · International*

## River Bistro

Frozen in time, which now makes it surprisingly cheap for a prime riverside spot — nothing special but perfectly acceptable.
