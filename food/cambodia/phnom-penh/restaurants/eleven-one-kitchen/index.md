A welcoming, simple restaurant in the residential area south of the Russian Market. They offer courtyard dining as well as a small air-conditioned dining room. A pleasant atmosphere and friendly service complement the good menu of local favourites. Prices are low by Phnom Penh standards and the food is standard Khmer — fish amok and beef salad — very well done. Cheap cold beer from the tap.
