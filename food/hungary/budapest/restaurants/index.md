# Restaurants in Budapest


## ILDIKÓ KONYHÁJA
Budapest · Hungarian Restaurant
Simple, tasty Hungarian comfort food at surprisingly good prices. The paprikás and pörkölt are both very good, especially with the homemade chilli condiment. No English menu.
→ [Read the full review](./ildiko-konyhaja/)

## MENZA ÉTTEREM ÉS KÁVÉHÁZ
Budapest · Hungarian Restaurant
Stands out for its attractive menu, which includes several goose dishes including roasted goose and foie gras. Very reasonable by international standards.
→ [Read the full review](./menza-etterem-es-kavehaz/)

## PAPRIKA VENDÉGLŐ
Budapest · Hungarian Restaurant
Can't go wrong here. A perfect place to sample traditional Hungarian food. The goose leg, topped with a chunky slice of fatty goose liver, is a must. My favourite restaurant in Budapest.
