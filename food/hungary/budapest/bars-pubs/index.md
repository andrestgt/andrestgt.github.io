# Bars & Pubs in Budapest


## BÁLNA TERASZ
Budapest · Bar & Terrace
The location by the Danube is basically impossible to beat. Cocktails by the carafe are surprisingly cheap, though a little weak.
→ [Read the full review](./balna-terasz/)

## ELLÁTÓ KERT & TAQUERIA
Budapest · Beer Garden
Nicely decorated hipster beer garden with reasonable prices and a Mexican theme.
→ [Read the full review](./ellato-kert-taqueria/)

## FEKETE KUTYA
Budapest · Pub
Really nice pub with decent prices and a selection of Hungarian beer snacks such as liver pâté and sausage.
→ [Read the full review](./fekete-kutya/)

## PÉPI SÖRÖZŐ
Budapest · Neighbourhood Pub
Good selection of beers and wines, and very tasty home-cooked meals. A very local place, no English spoken.
→ [Read the full review](./pepi-soeroezoe/)

## SZIMPLA KERT
Budapest · Ruin Pub
The best of the ruin pubs, quite an amazing place to visit. The house wine is inexpensive, though unfortunately served at room temperature.
→ [Read the full review](./szimpla-kert/)
