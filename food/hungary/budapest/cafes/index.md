# Cafés in Budapest


## GOSSIP COFFEE PLACE
Budapest · Café
A very nice-looking café, but the coffee does not justify the prices. Cappuccino is a lot for Budapest and arrives too hot and more bitter than it should be. Not recommended.

## TAMP & PULL ESPRESSO BAR
Budapest · Espresso Bar
Nice espresso bar out in the outskirts. The cappuccino is expertly prepared and cost 540 Ft — the best coffee I found in Budapest.
