# Bars & Pubs in Košice


## BARRIQUE – PIVOTÉKA & VINOTÉKA
Košice · Wine Bar
Nice little wine bar in the Old Town with an excellent white muscat by the glass for 3,60€ and a tasty strawberry Dunkel beer for 2,40€.
→ [Read the full review](./barrique-pivoteka-vinoteka/)

## THE BEER HOUSE
Košice · Beer Pub
Large beer pub with a huge range of beers on tap. Very fair prices for the Old Town location, with most large glasses costing 1,50€ or less.
→ [Read the full review](./the-beer-house/)

## DOBRÉ ČASY
Košice · Craft Beer Pub
Brilliant pub with lots of beers on tap, mostly craft beers, at surprisingly good prices. A must visit in Košice.
→ [Read the full review](./dobre-casy/)

## U SEBURGIHO
Košice · Pub
A pleasant surprise once you make your way through the drab courtyard and up the stairs. Good beer prices and an excellent duck pâté.
