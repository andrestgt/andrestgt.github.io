# Restaurants in Mallorca


## SA FARINERA
S'Horta · Restaurant
Beautiful restaurant with good house wine and genuinely excellent food — grilled rabbit, pig tongues, blood sausage, snails, and liver, all very well done. Probably the best food on Mallorca.
→ [Read the full review](./sa-farinera/)

## ANTIC CELLER SAN TOREÓ
Sineu · Restaurant
Traditional and good without being especially refined. The real highlight is the setting inside an ancient cellar. Portions are enormous and the house wine is good.
→ [Read the full review](./antic-celler-san-toreo/)

## ES CRUCE
Vilafranca de Bonany · Restaurant
Simple, honest food in huge portions at low prices, massively popular for good reason. The lechona is particularly good.
→ [Read the full review](./es-cruce/)

## RESTAURANTE SA FONA
Es Llombards · Restaurant
Simple village restaurant where the food is decent enough, although slightly overpriced for what it is. No paella at lunch out of season.
→ [Read the full review](./restaurante-sa-fona/)
