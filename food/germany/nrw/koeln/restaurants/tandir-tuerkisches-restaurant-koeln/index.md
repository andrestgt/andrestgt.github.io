An excellent Turkish restaurant serving really tasty, authentic food at very reasonable prices. Even the small meze platter is almost enough to fill two people, though that would be a shame, because the mains are excellent too, from the various charcoal-grilled kebabs to the lamb dishes.

The restaurant certainly won't win any awards for its décor, but it does have a courtyard with a terrace where you can sit comfortably outside in good weather.
