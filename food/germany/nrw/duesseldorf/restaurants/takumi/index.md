One of the most popular spots in Little Tokyo, and a place where the closely packed seating and open kitchen really do make you feel a little like you're in Asia. The ambience is rather plain and a bit cramped, but that seems to be part of the concept.

The speciality is the various kinds of ramen, and they are prepared really well here. Price-wise, it's at the higher end, but the quality is there.

A clear recommendation.
