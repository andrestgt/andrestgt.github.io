A gutbürgerlich Swabian restaurant in a beautifully renovated monastery barn in Bad Herrenalb. The atmosphere is appealing throughout. The menu offers fairly simple home-style cooking, but the quality is consistently fine. A recommended restaurant, especially if you're after a beautiful setting and classic, well-made Swabian cooking.
