Good god, this place is as old school as it gets. I had no ideas such places still exist nowadays, it's like time travelling to the 80s or even earlier. There didn't seem to be a single concession to the 21st century in the old fashioned dining room. Most patrons seemed way over 70, and the place was packed for lunch. The food and wine is 100% local Franconian. The sausages are excellent, good house white, too. The roasted pork was a bit bland, but I suppose they're worried about their main clientele's blood pressure. Unfortunately, they were out of Schäufele, which is the ultimate Franconian food, I bet it would have been good here
