I was both delighted and surprised to find such a great little Spanish tapas place in Illingen, run by an actual Spaniard. The patatas alioli, croquetas de bacalao, and tortilla were all solid. The larger meat and seafood dishes — sepia a la plancha, secreto ibérico — were even better.
