I was both delighted and surprised to find such a great little Spanish tapas place in Illingen, run by an actual Spaniard. The patatas alioli, croquetas de bacalao, and tortilla were all solid. The larger meat and seafood dishes were also excellent. One of the better restaurants in the entire region.
