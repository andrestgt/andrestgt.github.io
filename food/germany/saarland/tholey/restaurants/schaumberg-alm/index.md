High up on a hill in the lovely Sankt Wendel countryside, this place is built in the style of an Alpine hut. In good weather, the terrace offers fine views across the Saarland countryside. Prices for food and drink are fine, even if the portions for a hearty rural place could be a touch more generous. Recommended.
