# Restaurants

---

## Dicke Wirtin
*Charlottenburg · Traditional Berlin*
Good traditional Berlin food and atmosphere, but touristy and quite expensive. Worth a visit for the experience.
→ [Read the full review](/food/germany/berlin/restaurants/dicke-wirtin/)

---

## Konnopke's Imbiss
*Prenzlauer Berg · Currywurst*
A Berlin institution — excellent currywurst with a great topping sauce at an outstanding price-quality ratio.
→ [Read the full review](/food/germany/berlin/restaurants/konnopkes-imbiss/)

---

## Luftgarten
*Tempelhof · Beer Garden*
A unique beer garden on the disused Tempelhof airfield with loungers and a genuinely special setting — expensive, but unlike anything else.
→ [Read the full review](/food/germany/berlin/restaurants/luftgarten/)

---

## Song Nguu 'Wasserbüffel'
*Prenzlauer Berg · Vietnamese*
Possibly the best Vietnamese restaurant in Berlin — good, fresh food and fair prices. Highly recommended.
→ [Read the full review](/food/germany/berlin/restaurants/song-nguu/)

**Price level note:**
