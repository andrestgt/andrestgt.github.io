Compared with other huts nearby, the Klausentalhütte is on the modern side and doesn't exactly win you over with rustic charm. In good weather, though, you sit beautifully in the forest and the value for money is excellent. Portions are Pfälzer-generous and the local specialities tasty. There are very respectable wines at fair prices too, and the wine spritzers are poured with remarkable generosity.
