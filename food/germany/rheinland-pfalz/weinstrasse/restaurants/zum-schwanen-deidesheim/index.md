Zum Schwanen is among the less famous and pleasingly cheaper places in Deidesheim. The inner courtyard is particularly nice in summer. The cooking is classic, hearty Pfälzer fare — fried potatoes with blood sausage, Saumagen, and hearty stews. The Spätburgunder Rosé from Meßmer was very good and well priced. For the atmosphere alone, Zum Schwanen is a recommended stop on the Weinstraße.
