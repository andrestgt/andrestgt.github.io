At Alter Kastanienhof in the pretty village of Rhodt unter Rietburg, you can sit pleasantly in the courtyard in good weather. The gutbürgerlich cooking is solid enough, with seasonal dishes on offer. Service is very friendly and attentive. Mains tend toward €30 and above, which counts as upscale for the area.
