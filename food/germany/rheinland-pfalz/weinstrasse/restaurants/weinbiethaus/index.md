A pleasant excursion restaurant in the Pfälzerwald — though genuinely complicated to reach by car. The road is hair-raising in places. Prices are on the higher side for a Pfälzer excursion restaurant, but the food quality is solid, as you'd usually expect from the better Pfälzer establishments.
