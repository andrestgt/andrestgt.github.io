This forest hut with outdoor seating has a car park right by the road, which makes it very easy to reach. No wonder it's often busy. That's no problem, though, as there's plenty of seating and you can pretty much always find a free spot.

The tables and benches sit beneath large old trees, which makes the hut a perfect place in summer to sit in the shade. The usual Palatinate specialities taste very good here too, and the prices are absolutely reasonable.
