A remote Naturfreundehaus deep in the woods, reached on a lovely 45-minute walk along a stream from the car park. In theory you can drive up too, but only over a dusty track. It's precisely this secluded setting that gives the place its special charm.

There's a nice outdoor terrace, though it's better suited to the autumn or spring months, as it offers very little shade. The house wine comes from Weinbiet, which ensures good quality and value, and the Weinschorlen are very generously poured. The Palatinate food is excellent, even if my liver dumplings personally could have done with a bit more marjoram.
