Gaststätte Mühlengrund is set in deep countryside and is only reachable via narrow lanes. A lovely restaurant attached to an old mill with a beautiful garden terrace. The cooking is classic gutbürgerlich — brisket with horseradish, meat dumplings. Most mains are still under €20.
