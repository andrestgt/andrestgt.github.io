Alte Schmiede in the lovely wine village of Rhodt unter Rietburg is a typical Palatinate wine tavern, and in good weather it is pleasant to sit outside. The food quality is overall a notch above what you often get in simpler places in the region. The Leberknödel and the Saumagen were particularly good. Pricing seems a bit higher than at the forest huts in the area, but reaonably so: you get fried potatoes as a side, which you often have to pay extra for elsewhere, and you get table service rather than self-service. So overall you get better quality at a price level that, in the end, isn't far from the surrounding forest huts.
