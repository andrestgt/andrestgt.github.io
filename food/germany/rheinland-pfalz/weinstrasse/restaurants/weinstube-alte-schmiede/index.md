Alte Schmiede in the lovely wine village of Rhodt unter Rietburg is a typical Pfälzer wine tavern, and in good weather you can sit in the charming courtyard. The food quality is overall a notch above what you often get in simpler Pfälzer places. The Leberknödel and the Saumagen were both excellent.
