Unlike many other Pfälzer forest restaurants, this one is easy to reach by car and looks rather unassuming from outside. According to the menu, only organic ingredients are used. The food quality is genuinely excellent, as you'd usually expect from places that commit to organic sourcing. Well worth a visit.
