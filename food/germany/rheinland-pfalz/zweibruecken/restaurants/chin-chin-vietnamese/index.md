Chin Chin offers a full restaurant atmosphere — minimalist, modern and very well kept — combined with prices you'd more expect from a takeaway. The menu carries the usual Asian dishes supplemented by some more distinctly Vietnamese ones with fresh herbs. The service is on the grumpy side, but you can live with that. For the prices charged and an atmosphere well above what the prices suggest, it's a clear recommendation.
