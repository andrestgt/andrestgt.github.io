Chin Chin offers a full restaurant atmosphere — minimalist, modern and very well kept — combined with prices you'd more usually associate with a casual Asian takeaway. Value for money is accordingly very respectable. The menu carries the usual Asian-takeaway dishes you'd expect in Germany, supplemented by some more distinctly Vietnamese plates with fresh herbs. We tried both: classic duck of the kind you'd know from any Asian takeaway, and a Vietnamese soup. Both were genuinely good. The service is on the grumpy side, but you can live with that. For the prices charged, and an atmosphere well above what you'd expect from a takeaway, Chin Chin is hard to beat.
