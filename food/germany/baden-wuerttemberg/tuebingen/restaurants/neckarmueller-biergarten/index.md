The beer garden’s location by the river is its biggest draw, offering a fantastic setting for a relaxed outing. Unfortunately, the home-brewed beer is just average and doesn’t live up to the setting. The food options in the beer garden itself are limited and unappealing, so I didn’t try any. Come for the view, but don’t expect much from the menu.
