The smart, spacious dining hall of the brewery offers a fine selection of freshly tapped beer and hearty Swabian cooking. My favourite beer here is the unfiltered Engele, which I can wholeheartedly recommend if it's available. With a few lovely side dishes, it's an experience worth seeking out.
