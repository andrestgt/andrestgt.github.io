A gutbürgerlich Swabian restaurant in a beautifully renovated Klosterscheuer in Bad Herrenalb. The atmosphere is appealing and the setting is genuinely special. The menu offers fairly simple home-style cooking, but the quality is consistently fine. The Schwabenpfännle was hearty and well done. Overall, a recommended restaurant, especially if you're after a beautiful setting and classic, well-made Swabian cooking.
