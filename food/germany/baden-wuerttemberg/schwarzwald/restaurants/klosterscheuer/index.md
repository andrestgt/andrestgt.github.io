A traditional Swabian restaurant in a beautifully renovated manastery barn in Bad Herrenalb. The atmosphere is appealing and refined, without feeling overdone. The menu offers fairly simple home-style cooking, but the quality is consistently fine throughout. The Schwabenpfännle was nicely prepared and even contained a small but premium piece of beef. The meatloaf, too, was straightforward and tasty. Overall, a recommended restaurant, especially if you're after a beautiful setting and classic Swabian cooking.
