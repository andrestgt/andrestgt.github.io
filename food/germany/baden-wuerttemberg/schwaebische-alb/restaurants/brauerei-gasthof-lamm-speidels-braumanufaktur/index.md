What a pleasant surprise to find such a large and, above all, modern brewery inn in this remote village on the Swabian Alb. It's also good to see, and convenient, that the place is open seven days a week.

There's a good selection of house-brewed beers, from Weizen to IPA, which you can also buy bottled to take away. The menu isn't particularly creative, but the dishes are very good and the prices absolutely reasonable. The Spanferkelhaxe was even outstanding.
