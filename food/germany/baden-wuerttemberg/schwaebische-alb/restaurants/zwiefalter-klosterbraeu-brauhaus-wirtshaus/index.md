The smart, spacious dining hall of the brewery offers a fine selection of freshly tapped beer and hearty Swabian cooking in a modern setting, and is always worth a visit, practically a must if you're in the area. My favourite beer here is the unfiltered Engele, which I can wholeheartedly recommend if it's available. Some lovely, creamy wild garlic and cheese Spätzle on the side, and the calories you lost on the walk are right back in place.
