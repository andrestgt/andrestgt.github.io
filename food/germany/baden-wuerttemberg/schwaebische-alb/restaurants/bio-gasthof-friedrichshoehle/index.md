Friedrichshöhle is beautifully situated and can be reached on a pleasant 45-minute walk from Zwiefalten along a crystal-clear brook with trout in it. In good weather, you can sit outside and enjoy the lovely surroundings.

There is also good service and fine food. Of course, there is fresh trout, but also Swabian classics such as Maultaschen. Or you can simply stop here for coffee and cake, which is worthwhile too, as the cakes are excellent.

One of the most beautiful country restaurants I know on the Swabian Alb, and an absolute recommendation, even though prices have risen sharply in recent years, partly because of the organic quality, and the cappuccino on my last visit was unfortunately very poor.
