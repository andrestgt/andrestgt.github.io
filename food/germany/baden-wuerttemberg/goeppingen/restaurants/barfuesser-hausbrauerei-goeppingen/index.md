The concrete jungle of Göppingen's city centre is certainly not the most atmospheric setting for a brewpub. The interior, on the other hand, is very well done and creates a pleasant atmosphere. Unsurprisingly, the beer here is very good. Far more surprising were the Maultaschen with potato salad, which were of high quality for a restaurant chain.
