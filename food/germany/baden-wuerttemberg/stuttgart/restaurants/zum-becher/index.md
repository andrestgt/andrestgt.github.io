Zum Becher has apparently been around for a long time, even if I have to admit I'd never heard of it until recently. A Tuesday evening and it was extremely busy — booking is strongly recommended.

The menu sticks to the absolute classics of Swabian cooking. Pricing is absolutely within range for Stuttgart — certainly not cheap, but appropriate. The wine list has a good selection of regional wines. Overall, a clear recommendation for a refined, traditional restaurant with very good, friendly service.
