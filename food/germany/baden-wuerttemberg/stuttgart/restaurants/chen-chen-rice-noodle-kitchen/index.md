A small, modern canteen-style place where you really do feel like you're in Asia. The cooking is very authentic and offers a wide choice of dishes, from pig's ears and Sichuan chicken to braised pork belly and much more.

A nice touch is that you can put together your own platter from a selection of dishes for a varied meal. With dishes at around four euros each, roughly twelve euros gets you a full and varied meal.

Highly recommended for a small bite between meals, but also for a proper meal to share with friends.
