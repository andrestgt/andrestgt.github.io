A classic Besenwirtschaft in Obertürkheim, open only a few weeks a year. As you'd expect, it's almost always full, and waiting for a table is part of the deal.

House wines are fairly priced at €4 a Viertele and very respectable. The Maultaschen stand out: robustly filled, with noticeably real proportions of spinach, herbs and meat. Hard to complain.
