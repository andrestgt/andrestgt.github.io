# Restaurants

Stuttgart has a vibrant and varied restaurant scene, from upmarket Swabian wine taverns and creative international kitchens to excellent Asian restaurants and neighbourhood brewhouses.

---

## Athidhi Restaurant
*Gablenberg · Indian*
Outstanding Indian cooking from both north and south — the Mysore dosa and lamb curry are exceptional. An unreserved recommendation.
→ [Read the full review](/food/germany/baden-wuerttemberg/stuttgart/restaurants/athidhi/)

---

## Besenwirtschaft Rauscher
*Obertürkheim · Swabian Wine Tavern*
A classic seasonal wine tavern open only a few weeks a year — robustly filled Maultaschen and very fair wine prices. Expect to queue.
→ [Read the full review](/food/germany/baden-wuerttemberg/stuttgart/restaurants/besenwirtschaft-rauscher/)

---

## Biergarten Killesberg
*Killesberg · Beer Garden*
Pleasant beer garden in the greenery with decent Swabian cooking and generous portions at sensible prices.
→ [Read the full review](/food/germany/baden-wuerttemberg/stuttgart/restaurants/biergarten-killesberg/)

---

## Brauhaus Schönbuch
*City Centre · Brewhouse*
A good brewhouse atmosphere despite the backstreet setting, with a nice variety of beers and tasty Swabian food.
→ [Read the full review](/food/germany/baden-wuerttemberg/stuttgart/restaurants/brauhaus-schoenbuch/)

---

## Buschpilot
*Bad Cannstatt · Beer Garden*
A lovely beer garden in a large park, with hearty Swabian food and good Hefeweizen — very popular in good weather.
→ [Read the full review](/food/germany/baden-wuerttemberg/stuttgart/restaurants/buschpilot/)

---

## Carls Brewery
*City Centre · Brewery Restaurant*
Prime location overlooking Stuttgart's main square, with traditional Swabian food and good house-brewed beer. Hard to beat for a post-sightseeing meal.
→ [Read the full review](/food/germany/baden-wuerttemberg/stuttgart/restaurants/carls-brewery/)

---

## CQ Flavour
*Möhringen · Chinese*
The best Chinese restaurant in Stuttgart — authentic Sichuan and Northern Chinese cooking in an unlikely suburban location.
→ [Read the full review](/food/germany/baden-wuerttemberg/stuttgart/restaurants/cq-flavour/)

---

## Dilgelay – Der Stuttgarter Döner
*Bad Cannstatt · Döner*
One of the best classic döner in Stuttgart — a bit pricier than average, but the quality justifies it.
→ [Read the full review](/food/germany/baden-wuerttemberg/stuttgart/restaurants/dilgelay/)

---

## Four Brothers Burger
*Ostheim · Burgers*
A solid, consistent burger joint — nothing fancy, but does exactly what it says at a fair price.
→ [Read the full review](/food/germany/baden-wuerttemberg/stuttgart/restaurants/four-brothers-burger/)

---

## Goldener Adler
*West End · Swabian, Fine Dining*
One of the very best restaurants in Stuttgart — creative international starters paired with hearty traditional Swabian mains.
→ [Read the full review](/food/germany/baden-wuerttemberg/stuttgart/restaurants/goldener-adler/)

---

## Harmonie Griechisches Restaurant
*Ostheim · Greek*
A simple Gaststätte serving both Greek and local specialities — an oddity, but a pleasant one with decent food at reasonable prices.
→ [Read the full review](/food/germany/baden-wuerttemberg/stuttgart/restaurants/harmonie/)

---

## Huong Viet – Vietnamesische Spezialitäten
*Mitte · Vietnamese*
Authentic Vietnamese food in an unassuming East End restaurant — the Banh Da Cua and seafood Banh Xeo are highlights.
→ [Read the full review](/food/germany/baden-wuerttemberg/stuttgart/restaurants/huong-viet/)

## KRONE – Trattoria-Pizzeria
*Ostheim · Italian*
Really good Italian food in the east end with friendly service and prices well below what other Stuttgart Italian restaurants charge.
→ [Read the full review](/food/germany/baden-wuerttemberg/stuttgart/restaurants/krone-trattoria/)

---

## Martha's
*City Centre · International*
Fantastic location on one of Germany's most beautiful squares — quick, friendly service and perfectly decent food for a prime city-centre lunch.

A vast old-school feeding temple with an impressive buffet selection — uneven quality but suitable for a hungry group.

## Ristorante La Scala
*City Centre · Italian*
Good food but very high prices for modest portions — there are better-value Italian options in Stuttgart.
→ [Read the full review](/food/germany/baden-wuerttemberg/stuttgart/restaurants/ristorante-la-scala/)

## Sai Gon Asian Street Food
*Bad Cannstatt · Vietnamese*
Two menus — order from the Vietnamese one for the real deal. Rare dishes like Bun Bo Hue and Chao Long, at prices that reflect the quality.

## Tagesbistro Herr Kächele

A lunchtime-only Swabian bistro with excellent Maultaschen and the Schwabenteller at a very fair price.

## Tante Blanka – Höhengaststätte Neckarblick
*Obertürkheim · Swabian*
Nice views over the Neckar valley and good local food at affordable prices — Zwiebelrostbraten and Käsespätzle recommended.

## Taverna bei Kosta

A pretty Greek taverna in the greenery — generous mezze platters, tasty grilled dishes, and fairly reasonable prices for Stuttgart.

## Thai Palace Restaurant
