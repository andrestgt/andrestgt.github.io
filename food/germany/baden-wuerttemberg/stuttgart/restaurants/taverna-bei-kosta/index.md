A pretty taverna in the greenery on the outskirts, which we discovered fairly by chance since the other places nearby were all full.

What you get here is Greek hospitality, a generous mezze platter, and tasty grilled dishes at — for Stuttgart — fairly reasonable prices. Definitely a place to come back to.
