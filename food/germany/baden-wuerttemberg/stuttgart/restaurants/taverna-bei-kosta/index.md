A pretty taverna surrounded by greenery on the outskirts of the city, which we discovered largely by chance after all the other places nearby were packed. It turned out to be a very fortunate find.

What you get here is Greek hospitality, a generous mezze platter, and tasty grilled dishes at, by Stuttgart standards, fairly reasonable prices. The service is very friendly, and the atmosphere is pleasant and relaxed.

Definitely a place to come back to.
