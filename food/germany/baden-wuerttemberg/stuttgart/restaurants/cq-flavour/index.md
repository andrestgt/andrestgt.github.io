Despite its location in the backyard of a rather dreary shopping complex on the outskirts, CQ Flavour Restaurant is, for my money, the best Chinese restaurant in Stuttgart.

There are also the usual German-Chinese staples for less adventurous diners, and they do those perfectly well. But the real reason to come is the authentic Sichuan and Northern Chinese cooking. The interior still has that slightly dated German-Chinese restaurant look from the eighties, but the flavours are the real thing.
