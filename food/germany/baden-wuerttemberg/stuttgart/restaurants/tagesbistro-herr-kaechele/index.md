For Stuttgart, Tagesbistro Herr Kächele is well worth a visit, both in terms of price and quality. Worth knowing, though, that it is exclusively a lunchtime operation — dinner is not an option.

The food really is well done, especially the Maultaschen. The best option is the Schwabenteller, which gives you a bit of everything for a very fair price.
