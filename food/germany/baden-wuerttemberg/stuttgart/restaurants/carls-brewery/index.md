With its prime location, modern interior, and a very nice alfresco area overlooking Stuttgart's main square and castle, Carls Brewery doesn't need to try too hard. But it does.

The beer isn't overpriced, and the food, while not cheap, is traditional Swabian and very good. If you can find a seat, it's hard to think of a better place for a post-sightseeing meal in Stuttgart.
