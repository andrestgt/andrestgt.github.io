There are two menus here. The regular menu in German with standard Asia Imbiss items is fine. But the second menu — in Vietnamese — offers the real deal, and that's the reason to come.

There's a proper pho, Bun Bo Hue, and other rare Vietnamese dishes. Prices are quite high for such a basic place, but you pay for freshness and authenticity and that's justified.
