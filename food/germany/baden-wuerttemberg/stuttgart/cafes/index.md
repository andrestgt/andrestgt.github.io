# Cafés

---

## Café Lesbar
*City Library · Café*
Modern café on the top floor of the impressive city library, with fine views over the city and very good coffees. One of the nicest café settings in Stuttgart.
→ [Read the full review](/food/germany/baden-wuerttemberg/stuttgart/cafes/cafe-lesbar/)

## Caffè-Bar
*City Centre · Espresso Bar*
A rustic little espresso bar with an excellent flat white — exactly what a neighbourhood espresso bar should be.

## Coffreez Frozen Coffeebar
*Königstraße · Café*
Franchise with decent cappuccinos at €2.60 and a couple of comfy seats — a rare luxury on Königstraße.
