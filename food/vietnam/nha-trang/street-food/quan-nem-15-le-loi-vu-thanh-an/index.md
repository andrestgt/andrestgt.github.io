This is my favourite nem nuong place in Nha Trang, it's a noisy and busy simple eatery but the food is excellent. Nem nuong are this region's claim to fame when it comes to Vietnamese cuisine. Grilled pork paste with herbs, leaves, pickles, mango and the one ingredient which makes an already good dish special : crispy deep fried dough. Wrap all of this in rice paper, dip it in a special sauce of tomato, chili and minced pork, et voilà, heaven. They also do a good version of bun thit nuong here, grilled meat noodles.A portion of 6 nem nuong is just 50k, it used to be 40k just a few years back but it's still great value.
