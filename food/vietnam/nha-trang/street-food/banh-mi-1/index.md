I went there to try their Banh mi thap cam, the gold standard of the Vietnamese sandwich and my reference when comparing them. At #1 you get them generously filled with various meats and herbs and tasty sauce. The filling is excellent. Now the bread itself is good but not great, which is not their fault, most bakeries in Nha Trang are not up there with best in the country. There's only one bakery in town that makes baguettes as good as those you get in Hoi An and Da Nang. On a positive note they're better than the ones you get in Saigon.

Verdict : definitely one of the better banh mi in town and if you're in the tourist area, you can't go wrong here. 30k is obviously more than you'd pay in a local place, but considering the generous filling and location in tourist central, it's reasonable.

Update: quality is still good, but prices have gone up
