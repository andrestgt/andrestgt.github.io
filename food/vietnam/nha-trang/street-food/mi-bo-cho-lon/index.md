Very neat little noodle place that deserves to be a lot more popular. To my knowledge they're the only ones in Nha Trang who do Chinese influenced beef noodles. They have only a few dishes, with tripes or meat, as soup or dry, but they're all really delicious and great value. Big bowls go for 40-45k. A must visit for foodies in Nha Trang.
Edit : they just moved to bigger premises with a slightly more expansive menu and it's still delicious, plus they now serve homemade wonton.
