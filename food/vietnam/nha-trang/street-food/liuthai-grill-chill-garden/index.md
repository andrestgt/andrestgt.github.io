Simple street food place with small chairs and plastic tables on a busy road. So the Bangkok feeling is there.

Sadly, the food is not very good, and not very Thai.

The "tom yum" soup was a standard Vietnamese "lau thai", the seafood not exactly fresh.

The salads contain too much shrimp paste, but are a bit bland otherwise.

The cheesy minced pork skewers were quite good, but the only really nice flavour there, a real Thai flavour, was the chili makrut lime dip.

We paid over 400k which is too much for the quality
