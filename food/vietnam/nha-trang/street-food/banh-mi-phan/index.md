Along with Ba Le, this is the one of the better places for banh mi sandwiches in Nha Trang. They also do a few ones with a twist such as Indonesian chicken filling, but the regular thap cam is the best. They don't have the quality of what you get in the famous Hoi An eateries, but in Nha Trang right now, this is as good as it gets.

It used to be very touristy in the before times, but now with corona they survive very well on locals, a clear sign of quality
