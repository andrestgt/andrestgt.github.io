Cheap and cheerful, with tasty wraps starting from 50k. There's also some Greek appetisers, including a nice tirokafteri (80k). If you don't mind the less extensive menu and sitting on small chairs, you get pretty much the same quality here as in Mix (Nha Trang's premier Greek restaurant) but for less money, and you can bring your own booze, too. Cold craft beers are available at Moonmilk next door.

Keep in mind that this place can be extremely busy and since everything is freshly prepared, expect to wait
