Rustic beach shack with bean bags and chilled beer or coconuts. Best place to hang out on Nha Trang beach. 30k for a Saigon, 40k for a fresh coconut. That's a bit more than the basic places charge, but it's well worth paying a little extra for the right to chill on their bean bags. Dog friendly, too

Edit : change from 5 stars to 3 stars

I loved this place when they were in soft opening, but sadly since they had their official opening it has become overpriced (a can of Saigon costs roughly the same as the tasty craft beer at Louisiane) and they blast loud music even when it's empty around noon time, so the bean bags are quite useless for chilling.
