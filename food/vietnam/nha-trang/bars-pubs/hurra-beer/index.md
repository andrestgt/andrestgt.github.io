If you're nostalgic or just want to experience what a Vietnamese drinking place looked like in the 90s, Hurra delivers on both accounts.

Obviously, the selection of beers is a lot wider and the prices much higher than back then, but it recreates a nice vintage atmosphere all the way to even the background music.

The menu is varied with a few dishes that you don't see often, such as sour fish egg soup, and the quality of the cooking is better than your average quan nhau.

Beer prices are low with bottles between 14 and 25k, there's no draft beer though.

Count on spending 200k per pax for a good meal with a few beers.
