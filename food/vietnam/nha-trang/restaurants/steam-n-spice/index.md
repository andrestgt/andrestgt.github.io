If you're in Nha Trang and you feel a craving for Dim Sum you really don't have many options (although this is changing with the recent opening of Crystal Jade which I haven't tried yet). Steam n Spice is a mid-range restaurant in the Sheraton hotel. Their Dim Sum are of good quality and while not exactly inexpensive, still quite affordable at 3-5$ a basket. They do, however, grossly overcharge on drinks, so beware. The ice tea is not on the house, just like everywhere else in Vietnam, they actually charge 3$ for it.
