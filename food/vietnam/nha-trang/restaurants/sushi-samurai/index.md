This Western-owned place has a prime location in the tourist area, is surprisingly inexpensive, and looks good from the outside.

But unfortunately, you get what you pay for, really applies here.

We ordered some nigiri and a caterpillar roll, and ended up with a few dry pieces of boring rice and tasteless fish, and a small, good looking but completely bland eel roll with hardly any eel.

The poor service didn't help either to give me any other impression of this place than 'never again'.

Go to Kiwami instead, a bit more expensive, but light years ahead in terms of quality and atmosphere.
