An Italian restaurant run by Russians. But before you run away screaming, keep reading, please. This is actually one of the better places to eat in Nha Trang's tourist area. With its attractive dining room, attention to detail and beautifully looking dishes, you can forgive them that it's not an authentic Italian restaurant. If that's what you're looking for you better check out Da Fernando just opposite. You do get better value in a more upmarket atmosphere at Marinelli, though. The home made pasta dishes are very nice and the pizzas, while better at Fernando are pretty good, too, especially the toppings, the crust is too doughy for my taste. They have a few nice craft beers on tap as well as inexpensive sangria.
