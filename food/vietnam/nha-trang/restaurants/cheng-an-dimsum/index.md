The new premises are super nice and fancier than the prices would suggest. The quality of the food here is not exactly outstanding, but solid enough and good value. There are not many Dim Sum restaurants in town, and the best one in Sheraton is quite expensive, which makes this the best overall option.

Most dim sum dishes are around 50k.
