Started out just before Covid hit as an upmarket tourist restaurant, but is now often empty and showing signs of neglect.
Having said that, Golden Bamboo still offers a good value menu of Vietnamese and international food, a nice looking dining room and dirt cheap cold Saigon beer (10k).
The quality of the food is nothing special, but perfectly adequate for the price.
