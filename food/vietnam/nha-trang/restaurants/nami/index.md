One of the most attractive of the new breed of Korean restaurants that have mushroomed in Nha Trang recently. The service is very friendly and each table have their own grill. Appetisers are good and generous. They have a nice variety of meat, including very tender marinated beef. Expect to pay around 200 per head for a filling BBQ (this is obviously not a place for solo diners), more if you go for the fancier beef cuts.

My only criticism would be the lack of none meat bbq dishes, there's no pancakes, tteokbokki, mandus, and practically no stews.

So if you're only looking for a good Korean barbecue restaurant, this place is recommended, if you'd like a more varied Korean meal, there's other options around
