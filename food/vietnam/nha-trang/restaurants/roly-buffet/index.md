As someone who is not a fan of buffet restaurants, I must admit that I quite liked Roly. It's a huge and busy place with a rather sterile atmosphere, but quality and value are both good.

190k with unlimited beer, there's lots of marinated meat and seafood which you can grill right on your table and a hotpot, too.

The freshly prepared banh xeo are quite nice, but the precooked dishes are not being kept warm at all, so it's best to avoid them and go straight for the BBQ and hotpot options.
