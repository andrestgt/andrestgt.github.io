La Casserole is a small, unpretentious restaurant in the tourist area. It's quite popular with expats, but also worth finding out for visitors to Nha Trang.

The food is essentially Swiss but with a few Asian dishes thrown in for good measure.

My favourite dish there is actually an Asian style tuna carpaccio which is delicious and very generous for 4$.

Western classics like antipasti platter or croque monsieur are tasty and reasonably priced.

They only have one choice of wine, which is a bit lacking, but their house wine, while on the pricier side, is very decent quality.

I would have given 5 stars, but on my last visit the cordon blue was slightly overcooked and a little dry.

I'm sure that was just a small mistake on that day, but i only give 5 stars for flawless food.

Nevertheless, i highly recommend this friendly place, and i will certainly return too.
