I'd heard great things about 4P from other people, but it took me a long time to check out what's the hype all about. The main reason was that I'm generally sceptical of restaurants that are located in hotels, especially luxury hotels, since they're often mediocre and overpriced. 4P is different though. It's obviously not a budget eatery, but for the quality they offer here, the prices are surprisingly moderate. The pizzas are excellent, hands down the best in Nha Trang, toppings and cheeses are high quality and the dough is baked to perfection in a wood fired oven. You can order most pizzas with their homemade burrata on top, but I would advise against it. The burrata is delicious, but it's better to order it as a side dish with some bread, rather than on the pizza. Another great side dish is their small cheese fondue which is a steal for around 100k. Most pizzas are around 200k.

They serve craft beers from the tap for less than you'd pay in most local bars (75k+) and there's a happy hour between 11 am and 6 pm.
