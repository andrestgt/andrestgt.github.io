After choices for Korean food had been very limited for many years, BBQ places are now mushrooming all over Nha Trang. This place has a good location near Xom Moi Market close to the tourist area. It's a basic place with outdoor seating downstairs and an air conditioned dining room upstairs where everybody sits. All guests except us were Koreans which is generally a good sign. The place is super authentic, even the menu is Korean and Vietnamese only. There are nice appetisers, delicious Kimchi and very generous portions of barbecue meat. The prices are very reasonable for the amount of food you get, 150-200k per head will feed you well. The reason I only give 4 stars is the lack of variety in the menu, there's basically only pork available, no other meat cuts, no seafood, no mandus. So come here for a cheap and cheerful pork barbecue, if you want a more extensive Korean food experience, go to Kimchi around the corner
