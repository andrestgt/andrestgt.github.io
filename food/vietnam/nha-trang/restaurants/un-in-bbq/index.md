One of my favourite and best places in NT but for a five star review it lacks consistency.

I eat here often and sometimes the food is simply gorgeous. Perfect spicy, fall-off-the-bone ribs, huge portions, fair prices.

At other times, however, the ribs are still chewy and the portions are smaller.

I'm not sure why this happens because Un In has the potential to be the best value, best loved place to eat in town.

I still wholeheartedly recommend the place, it's deservedly popular with locals and foreigners alike, which is rare in Nha Trang. The prices are really fair, even on an off day you still get great value and the fact that it's located by the river, outside of the main tourist area only adds to the appeal.

I also recommend ordering some of their Vietnamese dishes as sides, the anchovy salad or bo la lot, they're as good here as in most local places.
