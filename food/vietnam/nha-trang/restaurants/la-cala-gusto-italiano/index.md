For many years there was only one real Italian restaurant in Nha Trang, Da Fernando, along with a few faux ones, the likes you find in all tourist cities worldwide. Fortunately, Fernando now has competition from this excellent place in the North of town. And La Cala wins hands down. It's not only a much more tasteful place decor wise, but they also excel in the food department while charging similar prices. The pizzas from the wood fired oven are excellent. They would be the best in town if it wasn't for 4p at Sheraton, which is not a real comparison though since 4p is a pizza specialty place and not an Italian restaurant and also quite a bit pricier. At La Cala the pizzas go for 150-200k which is OK for the quality. I also loved the insalata di polpo which tasted like in Italy and the bruschette with home made oven baked bread. Only criticism would be the waiting time for food, it took an hour or so to get our first dish, but fortunately there's some good wines to pass the time
