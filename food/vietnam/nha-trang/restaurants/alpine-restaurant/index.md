I came here for a quick lunch and was very pleasantly surprised by the tranquil atmosphere and friendly service. Alpine reminded me of time when Nha Trang‘s tourist area was blessed with almost a dozen very good, affordable French restaurants.

The prices for food are very reasonable and the dishes look very attractive with sides of roasted vegetables and spinach. The cordon bleu (135k) comes with a nice truffled cream sauce. Sadly, they only offer a chicken option while I prefer the more flavourful pork or veal cordon bleu. The mashed potatoes could have been a bit fluffier, but at least they didn't taste of rancid margarine like in some other restaurants around.

A bottle of Saigon goes for 19k.

All in all, despite some room for improvement, a good meal at a great price
