This microbrewery on a quiet residential street near Co-op mart is a great find that deserves to be a lot more popular. They have quite a nice selection from IPA, to heavy stout, to fruity fresh ginger beer, and they all taste like proper beers, not a given in Vietnam in this price bracket ( most glasses under 30k). Add to that a menu of very reasonably priced drinking food such as snail rolls, smoked duck breast or roasted pork belly with galangal and you have a winner. It's a 15 min walk or short cab ride from the tourist area and I promise you won't regret this trip.
