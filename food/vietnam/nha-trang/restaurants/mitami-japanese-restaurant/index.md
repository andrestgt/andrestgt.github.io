Mitami is a good friendly Japanese restaurant in the tourist area that has only one problem. It's just around the corner from Kiwami, which is one of the best Japanese restaurants I know and a firm favourite of mine. So while Mitami is doing a great job and has cheaper prices, they just can't compete with the best. Still, it's better than Sakura and much better than Oh Sushi and Samurai, so I'd recommend it wholeheartedly to anyone who wants quality Japanese food at very reasonable prices. Their wine list is more affordable than at most other restaurants in Nha Trang, as well, which is a bonus.
