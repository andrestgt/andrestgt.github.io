Popular local restaurant north of the river, mostly frequented by local birthday or corporate parties. But don't let that put you off, it's quite a quintessential Vietnam experience and the food in such places is generally very good. As it here, especially their specialty dish : Chan Gio Heo Muoi, a special kind of roasted pork. Mid range prices, count on spending 250-300k for 2 with beers
