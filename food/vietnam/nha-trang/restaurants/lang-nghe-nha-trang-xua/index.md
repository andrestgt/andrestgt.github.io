Nha Trang Xua seems like a very secret, off the beaten track kind of place, quite impossible to find, far away from the tourist area.

But the truth is, everyone in Nha Trang knows this place, and so do the tour guides, so don't expect to come here for some unique local dining experience.

Having said that, it's a gorgeous place with its quaint ambiance and the beautiful Lotus pond. The traditional Vietnamese food is generally very good and reasonably priced.

If you're tired of the city, this is a great place for a nice meal in wonderful surroundings and I'd certainly recommend it.

But if you want something truly local and even better food in equally beautiful surroundings, then try some of the restaurants on the river, no English menus there, though
