I’ve always wondered why this basic little coffee shop on Biet Thu is so popular. Every time I pass by, it’s packed with both tourists and locals, despite the tiny chairs and plastic tables on the sidewalk (and the seating inside isn’t much better).

Well, the answer is simple: value!

You get a very good cappuccino for 32k and a Vietnamese coffee for under 20k, a great price for a spot right in the heart of the tourist area.
