The newest Iced Coffee location in town is a bit smaller than others with less comfy seating, but it's also less busy. Coffees are still good, the Vietnamese as well the espresso drinks.

I'll always have a soft spot for this franchise since they were the first, even before Highlands, to serve good and affordable espresso drinks in Nha Trang more than a decade ago.
