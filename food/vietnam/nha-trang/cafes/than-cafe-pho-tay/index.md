It seems like half of the reviews here are about the cocktail bar rather than the café. I've never been here in the evening, so I can only comment on the coffee. Their cappuccino is hands down the best in the tourist area of Nha Trang. That said, it’s actually more like a flat white, with really nice microfoam and an excellent slightly acidic espresso flavour. At 48k, I don’t think you can get anything better in town. The seating isn’t very comfortable, but at least they have a few chairs on the street, as well as an air-conditioned space inside
