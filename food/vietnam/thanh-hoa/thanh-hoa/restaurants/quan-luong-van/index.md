Thanh Hoa's most popular Bia hoi joint is worth a visit for the local specialty Nem nuong, which is grilled fermented pork and very fresh beer from the tap. They also do a surprisingly tasty warm beef tripe and starfruit salad. Not as cheap as it looks though. Most dishes are 80-100k.
