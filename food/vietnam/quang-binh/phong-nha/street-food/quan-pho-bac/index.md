Considering the name of this place simply means Northern style beef noodle soup eatery, I never expected to experience one of the my most memorable meals in Vietnam here. Obviously, the views over the river are stunning, but at heart this is just a simple Pho joint in the morning and a drinking place at night. Guests sit on small plastic chairs and the bia hoi comes in 2 litre kegs. But what makes this really special is their smoked roasted duck, the best I've had in Vietnam, with crisp skin, juicy meat and a delicious taste. They only do a few ducks every day, so if you can, reserve yours in advance
