This is an attractive expat hangout, but I wasn't entirely convinced mainly because the food was a bit disappointing and the happy hour was only for a very limited selection of bland industrial beers. The trio of dips that we ordered was OK taste wise, but it was a tiny portion of actual dips (mostly bread, carrot and cucumber) and rather pricy at 80k.
