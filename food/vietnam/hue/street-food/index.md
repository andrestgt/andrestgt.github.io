# Street Food

## Bánh Bèo Nậm Lọc Bà Đỏ
*Huế*
Simple garden restaurant with a short, focused menu of Huế specialities; not quite street food cheap, but hard to beat for the money...
→ [Read the full review](/food/vietnam/hue/street-food/banh-beo-nam-loc-ba-do/)

---

## Bánh Ép Ngon Rẻ
*Huế*
Vietnam's answer to a burrito, essentially a pressed pancake wrap with various fillings; undeniably tasty and dirt cheap...
→ [Read the full review](/food/vietnam/hue/street-food/banh-ep-ngon-re/)

---

## Bún bò O Ty
*Huế*
A friendly local neighbourhood place selling excellent bun bo bowls for a mere 25k; not one of Hue's overpriced tourist joints...
→ [Read the full review](/food/vietnam/hue/street-food/bun-bo-o-ty/)

---

## Bún Giấm Nuốc
*Huế*
For completists; a rarely found bowl of noodles with jellyfish and prawn crackers, only available in summer...
→ [Read the full review](/food/vietnam/hue/street-food/bun-giam-nuoc/)

## Com Hen Hoa Dong
*Huế*
Not to be confused with its namesake further down the alley; perfectly decent mussel rice for very little money...
→ [Read the full review](/food/vietnam/hue/street-food/com-hen-hoa-dong/)

## Cơm hến Hoa Đông
*Huế*
Considered the best Com Hen on Hen Island, and the most popular too; a regular bowl is just 10k...
→ [Read the full review](/food/vietnam/hue/street-food/com-hen-hoa-dong-2/)

## Quán Nướng Cay 202
*Huế*
Cheap as chips and really good food; some seriously nice grilled marinated meat dishes at rock bottom prices...
→ [Read the full review](/food/vietnam/hue/street-food/quan-nuong-cay-202/)

## Quán nướng Thủy
*Huế*
Really good grilled chicken, duck and bo la lot; a bit more expensive than expected, but hard to beat for quality grilled meat...

## Venus Beer Garden

More upmarket than A Luoi's other eateries, with a good varied menu of drinking food including rare items like raw beef salad...
