Tourist restaurant in a prime backpacker central location. We were attracted by their German style bia hoi. This being corona time, however, they can't sell fast enough and the cask was off. Still, they do Huda on tap for 18 k which is alright. The food is clearly for tourists only and not the kind of quality I'm used to but, you can't fault them for it. The Banh khoai (59k) was perfectly acceptable, just rather unremarkable, and came without a whole lot of herbs and leaves, without banh trang to roll and a rather tame peanut sauce lacking a punch of mam tom and chili.
