For a good reason this is considered the best Com Hen (baby mussels rice) place on Hen Island, and it's the most popular too. Prices, however, are still very cheap, a regular bowl is 10k and a bigger bowl with the lot goes for 15k. They also do a Banh Beo platter which puts some of the fancier Hue cake restaurants in town to shame and for the fraction of a price (20k for a platter). I'd highly recommend this place to sample some of Hue's special foods.
