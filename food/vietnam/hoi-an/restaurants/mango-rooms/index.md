Sadly prices here have gone through the roof since my first visit in 2006. But if that's not an issue, it's still a great change from all the same same Hoi An specialty eateries. Top notch fusion food, although this kind of cuisine has become rather unfashionable in recent years.

Happy hour cocktails are a good deal, quality concoctions, from noon until 7 pm
