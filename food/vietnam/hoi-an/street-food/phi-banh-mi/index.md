If the big boys Phuong and Madam Khanh are too busy, or if you simply want a great baguette sandwich at a local price from friendly people, then Phi is possibly the best option. A fully stuffed thap cam goes for 15k, which as of July 2020 that's half the price of Phuong or Khanh. Mind you, it's not quite up there in terms of deliciousness, but it's still better than most banh mi you'll get outside the Danang/Hoi An region, including some famous ones in Hanoi and Saigon.
