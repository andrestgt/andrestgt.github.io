Very attractive restaurant in an old converted French villa. Some of their dishes are spectacular, such as the ca long la lot. However, the fish they use for their eponymous cha ca, catfish, while perfect in texture, has a slightly musty river taste to it, which costs them the fifth star. Prices at Chaka are very reasonable for the ambiance and quality
