I've been coming here for almost two decades. Not only does Quán An Ngon have a stunning setting in a converted French villa, but it's the ideal place to sample various street foods from all over the country in nice, clean surroundings. The quality used to be impeccable and prices reasonably low. However, as with many famous and popular places in Vietnam, it seems that while the prices keep going up, quality control is not exactly a priority. While the food is still good these days, they'll need to try a bit harder to make it proper Ngon again, or maybe not, coz the place is packed every night anyway
