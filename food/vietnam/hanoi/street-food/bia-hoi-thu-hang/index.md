Seriously, visiting this huge beer garden is a more quintessential Hanoi experience than the bia hoi Junction. It's everything that is so amazing about travelling in Vietnam in general, and Hanoi in particular. And I understand that it could be a bit intimidating at first to visit a place so packed and frenetic, with the busy bike parking lot taking up almost half the beer garden. But don't let that stop you, the place is amazing. As soon as you sit down, they'll serve you a glass of ice cold and refreshing bia hoi (13k a mug but it's good quality bia hoi) and they won't stop serving them until you tell them to. The food is great, but it's served only in huge portions meant for sharing, so if you're alone stick with beer, peanuts and nem in banana leaf, or order maximum one dish. So whether you're a traveller looking for an authentic experience or an expat taking visitors, check out this place, you won't regret it
