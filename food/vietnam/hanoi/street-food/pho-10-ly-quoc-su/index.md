Most famous Pho place in the Old Town so you can imagine it's packed with locals and tourists alike most of the time. But generally it doesn't take too long to get a table and service staff are quite helpful. The Pho is obviously Northern style, with coriander and very clear broth. I personally prefer my broth a little more pungent, Southern style, but there's no denying that this is a very fine bowl of Pho, and coriander always makes me happy. 60k, a reasonable price for a bowl of Pho of this quality.
