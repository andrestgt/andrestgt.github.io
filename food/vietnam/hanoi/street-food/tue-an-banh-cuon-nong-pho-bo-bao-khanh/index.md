My go to place for banh cuon in Hanoi for almost two decades. Almost unchanged since then, it's still cheap and cheerful. Price as of 2020 is 25k a plate. Their home made dumplings are nice too. For something a bit more substantial, try their eel noodles.
2022 update : it's now 35k and they no longer seem to do the eel noodles. Still a good place
