This place unfortunately gives a disastrous first impression. It looks forlorn and slightly run down. There are no bar stools for solo guests, only large uncomfortable tables. The one single staff is constantly on her phone, not giving the slightest attention to customers. They have beer flights in the menu which they say they don't do anymore without giving any reasons and it doesn't make any sense since all the beers were available on tap, you might as well just pour them into smaller glasses and let your customers sample the beers you have on offer.

Apparently there's food, too, but I wasn't even given a food menu, so I'll never know.

Now the reason I'm still giving 3 stars is that the beers are actually really good*, with a nice variety from fruity refreshing lagers to heavy IPAs, and at the prices they charge, it might be the best value craft beers in Vietnam.

That's why it's such a pity that they don't seem to have a capable management to properly run this place.

*Well, there's one exception, do not, under any circumstances, think about ordering their pomelo beer, it tastes like, I would imagine, if you mix sparkling water with dishwashing liquid, anyway, you've been warned
