Infiniti is hands down the island's nicest place for a good coffee, with its chic modern décor, air conditioning, and good Wi-Fi.

There's a rather pricey menu of international dishes, but we only ever came here for coffee. In fact, this became our daily coffee stop because it's the best place in town for that. They do very nice Vietnamese-style coffees, including cà phê dừa, or coconut coffee, as well as pretty decent espresso drinks.
