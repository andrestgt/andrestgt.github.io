This is another one dish only ice cream shop, new and less famous than the one up the road where all the tourist cars stop.

The one dish they sell is Coconut ice cream, an island specialty, and as delicious as it sounds.

What I don't understand though : this place has the most wonderful location in a large shady palm grove and yet there's no beverages, no coffee, no beers, it feels like such a waste of prime space.

Price here is 60k for a cup, more expensive than up the road for the same quality and quantity, but if atmosphere is more important than price, I'd recommend coming here.
