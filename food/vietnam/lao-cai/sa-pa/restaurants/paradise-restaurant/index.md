One of the few old traveller cafés still standing after Covid19, Paradise offers spectacular views of Fansipan and the rice terraces. The food is very good tourist fare at acceptable prices. Dining out in Sapa used to be incredibly cheap but those days are long gone, now it's one of the most expensive places for food in Vietnam. They do excellent pork spring rolls for 60k. A Lao Cai Beer is 20k. And then there's the views, which is the fifth star
