This is an attractive restaurant with an extensive mouth watering menu at extremely reasonable prices. Sitting upstairs is lovely and service is quite attentive.

The portions are good, the quality of the ingredients perfectly fine.

For the prices they charge, this could be an absolute winner.

Unfortunately, there's one small problem. The dishes that come out of the kitchen are just not convincing.

They could let any random Vietnamese mama cook the food for them, and I'm sure the result would be so much better. As it is, most dishes simply taste bland, as if they're trying to cook for Western palates, completely unaccustomed to Asian food.

But is there anyone who really enjoys to eat this kind of food, I wonder.

After adding copious amount of fish sauce and chilies, all the food that came out was okay, but that's just not good enough in a city full of amazing food. Pity.
