Throughout South East Asia, the Crystal Jade franchise stands for high quality dumplings, and its only outlet in Vietnam is no exception to the rule.

It's a particularly upmarket affair located on the premises of a 5 star hotel, but the prices for Dim Sum are quite reasonable, three people can get stuffed for less than 500k.

The selection is not as extensive as in some other CJ outlets but the usual supects, such as Hakao, Siomai, Xiao Long Bao are all there and all excellent.

The big drawback is that they only serve Dim Sum between 11am and 2.30pm and the service is too obstrusive for my taste.
