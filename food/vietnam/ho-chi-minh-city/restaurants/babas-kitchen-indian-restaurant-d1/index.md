I will have to go with the minority opinion on this one and say that the Indian food here is far from excellent.

I didn't actually eat there but had a delivery which arrived well on time and still hot, so freshness can't be the problem.

The mutton samosas were the best dish, crispy outside, nice lamb inside, with a decent dip, nothing wrong with that.

The curries, however, did not convince me at all. The mutton korma and the malai kofta were overpowered by cardamon and bland otherwise.

The chicken tikka masala had a peculiar undefinable smoky note to it, which overpowered the masala and the meat.

The portions are generous and price is okay but i don't rate this place, there's quite a few really good Indian restaurants in HCMC, it's easy to give this one a miss.
