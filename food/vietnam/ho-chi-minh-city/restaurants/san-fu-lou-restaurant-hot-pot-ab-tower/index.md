This Cantonese restaurant in a prime location is great news for Dim Sum lovers in HCMC. With an extensive menu of the small goodies and long opening hours (21 hours), it lets you satisfy your dumpling cravings day or night, particularly if you're looking for high quality Dim Sum.

Some of them are outright divine, such as the duck and truffle dumplings, but the standard ones are very good too, not quite as excellent as Crystal Jade, but close and much easier to avail of.

The only small disappointment were the Xiao Long Bao, which were a little doughy, but still good.

Prices are reasonable for such an upmarket restaurant, with baskets of 3 ranging from 45-65k, although beverages are overpriced.

I will definitely keep coming back here, although they charge 10% VAT on top of the menu prices, a practice which I detest, so I'll deduct one star
