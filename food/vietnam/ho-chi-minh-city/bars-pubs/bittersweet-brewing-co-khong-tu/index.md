If I could design my perfect restaurant for a night out, it would look a lot like this one. A beer buffet with plenty of home-brewed craft beers to choose from, including some truly excellent ones, all for just 229K. That’s an unbelievable price for the quality.

You’d expect a place like this to make up for the cheap beer with overpriced food, but that’s not the case here. The food is reasonably priced and actually very good.

My favorite beers were the Mango Chili Hazy IPA, which was surprisingly fruity, something I didn’t expect, but still super refreshing and tasty. I also loved the Cream Ale, which was just perfect.

It’s not just a decent spot, it’s a really great spot for a night out, and I’m a little surprised it’s not a lot more popular. If this place were in District 1, the prices would probably be double for this kind of quality.

It’s just a 10-minute walk from the metro station, and the ride from District 1 takes only 20 minutes. So, anyone with a little bit of time to spare should totally visit this place.
