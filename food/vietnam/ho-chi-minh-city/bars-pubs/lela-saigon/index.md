In theory this is a place to love and be happy it exists. A proper live music bar with a competent band, a good atmosphere and a perfect downtown location. So much more pleasant than the horrible noise pollution beer clubs that have taken over Bui Vien.

Unfortunately it all doesn't add up, especially taking into consideration the sky high prices for drinks that they're charging. Given that there's no entrance fee, it's perfectly acceptable for a live music venue to charge extra for their drinks but beer and cocktails at 180k would only be reasonable if they had excellent bands playing here, not a second rate cover band with a male vocalist with a good rock voice but an English pronunciation that made my ears bleed, and female vocalists with good English but unsuitable RnB voices (this is supposed to be a rock bar after all). Seeing them I thought about the Philippines where you find better bands than this in much less expensive venues. As a matter of fact, for the price of a cocktail pitcher here (4 small glasses) you can almost get a one way ticket to Manila, which may be money better spent.
