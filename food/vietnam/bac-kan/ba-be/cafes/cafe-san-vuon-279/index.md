If we were anywhere south of the Northern mountain area, I wouldn't even review this place, let alone give five stars. It's pretty much your standard Vietnamese garden café of which there are usually dozens in most towns, and generally at least a couple in most villages in the south. In this area, however, this place is an oasis in the desert. Not only are there hardly any cafés at all within 50 kilometres, this one even serves real, good quality Daklak coffee by filter at a low price  (20k with or without milk and ice). Plus, it's friendly, there's plugs, sofas and WiFi.
