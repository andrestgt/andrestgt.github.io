There's a good reason why Yummy is the most popular traveller café in Cat Ba. It's friendly, inexpensive and the food is good, and better value than many of the places that cater to local tourists. The food is mostly Vietnamese with a few Thai and Western dishes thrown in, with lots of backpacker favourites. The Vietnamese dishes are tasty and authentic, and even most seafood dishes cost less than 100k, a bottle of Haiphong Bia is 15k, but there's also good bia hoi for 9k a mug.
