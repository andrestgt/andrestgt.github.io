Very nice little café in the Old Town. It seems brand new and as such less busy than the other ones in the area. But it's perfect for a Haiphong coffee break. The do great espresso drinks at very low prices. A cup of cappuccino is only 35k. There's ac, fast WiFi and friendly English speaking service
