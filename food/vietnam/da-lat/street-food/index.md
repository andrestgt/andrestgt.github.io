# Street Food

*Đà Lạt*
Chicken rice noodles, one of Dalat's must-try specialties; perfectly balanced fish sauce dressing, neither too salty nor too sweet, a real steal at 20k...

## Bánh xèo Hải sản ngon nhất Đà Lạt
*Đà Lạt*
Simple, popular neighbourhood eatery doing one thing well; crispy 'Phan Rang' style seafood pancakes, nicely balanced fish sauce, 8-10k a piece...

## Bột chiên A Tỉ
*Đà Lạt*
Filling vegetarian meal for very little money, an ideal cold weather dish for Dalat's chilly evenings; less refined but tasty, filling and cheap...

## Bún Bò Thủy

Simple family eatery with a different soup each day; excellent flavourful broth and crunchy leaves, very inexpensive at 25-30k a bowl...
