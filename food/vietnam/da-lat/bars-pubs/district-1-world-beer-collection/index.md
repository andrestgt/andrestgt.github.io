This would be a fantastic place with tasty craft beers at good prices. It's perfectly located by the market and has attentive service staff.

But unfortunately I would never come back here again. The music is blasted out so loud that it destroys eardrums and makes any conversation impossible.

That's acceptable in a dance club but not a beer pub with food.
