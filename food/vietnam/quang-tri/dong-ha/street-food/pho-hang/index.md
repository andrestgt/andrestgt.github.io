This neat, modern eatery serves very good bowl of Northern style Pho (both beef and chicken) at very moderate prices, a large Dac Biet including a generous amount of delicious soft beef tendons goes for 35k. Plus there's a few dry noodle dishes, such as Pho Tron which are equally tasty. A good choice if you find yourself in Dong Ha for some reason.
