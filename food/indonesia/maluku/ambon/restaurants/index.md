# Restaurants in Ambon

A selection of restaurants and seafood places in and around Ambon, mostly concentrated along the bay in Teluk Ambon.

---

## RESTAURANT SARI GURIH BEACH
Lateri, Baguala · Seafood, Indonesian
The best seafood restaurant around Ambon — a huge place on stilts above the water with excellent ikan kuah kuning and great views over the bay.
→ [Read the full review](./restaurant-sari-gurih-beach.html)

## SARI GURIH RESTAURANT
Honipopu, City Centre · Seafood, Indonesian
The city-centre branch of Sari Gurih — same menu, less appealing setting, but still a solid choice if you're staying in town.
→ [Read the full review](./sari-gurih-restaurant.html)

## TELUK AMBON BEACH CAFFE & RESTO
Rumah Tiga, Teluk Ambon · Seafood, Indonesian
A large open-air restaurant by the sea with good food and very reasonable prices — a good option for a relaxed lunch by the water.
→ [Read the full review](./teluk-ambon-beach-resto.html)

## WAILELA CAFE AND RESTO
Rumah Tiga, Teluk Ambon · Seafood, Indonesian
One of the most popular seaside restaurants around Ambon, with wide views over the bay and good portions at fair prices.
→ [Read the full review](./wailela-cafe-resto.html)
