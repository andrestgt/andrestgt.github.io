We stopped at Barista Coffee near Ambon harbour shortly after arriving on the Pelni ferry. The place is conveniently located close to the port and served what were probably the prettiest cappuccinos I saw in town, nicely presented and clearly made with some attention to appearance.

The coffee itself was less convincing. I had to stop the staff from putting cinnamon on my cappuccino, and the milk was served far too hot. It was drinkable, but not among the best cups I had in Ambon.

Still, if you need a coffee near the harbour after arriving by ferry, Barista Coffee is a convenient place for a quick stop.
