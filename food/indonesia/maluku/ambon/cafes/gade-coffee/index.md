Gade Coffee & Gold was my regular morning coffee stop in Ambon for the simple reason that it opens early, which is unusual for coffee places in the city.

The coffee is a strong roast and costs 31K. It is not specialty coffee in the same sense as some of the newer cafés in town, but it is good, strong and does the job perfectly in the morning.

The place was always empty when I went there early, which made it a convenient and quiet spot for a morning coffee when most other cafés are still closed.
