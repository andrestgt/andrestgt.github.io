# Cafés

Coffee options on Banda Neira are limited but improving, with Serenade bringing proper espresso drinks to the island.

---

## Cilu Bintang Estate
*Banda Neira · Café & Bar*
The most atmospheric spot in Banda Neira — a second-floor veranda with superb views toward the fort and Gunung Api, excellent fruit juices, and a very good buffet dinner when the chef is in town.
→ [Read the full review](/food/indonesia/maluku/banda-islands/cafes/cilu-bintang-estate/)

---

## Serenade Food & Beverage
*Banda Neira · Café*
The best coffee in Banda Neira, with surprisingly decent espresso drinks at 20K — only open from 5 pm, which is its main limitation.
