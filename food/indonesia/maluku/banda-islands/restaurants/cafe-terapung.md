Cafe Terapung serves the best food I had on Banda Neira. The place is hidden at the end of a narrow alley in the fishermen's neighbourhood, and the restaurant itself sits on stilts above the water. There is no printed menu.

The only thing to do is order the ikan bakar. The fish arrived perfectly grilled and coated with a delicious sambal. It was served with side dishes of aubergine and water spinach, and even these simple sides tasted noticeably better than elsewhere on the island.

We paid about 250K in total for three grilled fish dishes with all the sides.

Simple place, excellent cooking, and easily the best meal I had in Banda Neira.
