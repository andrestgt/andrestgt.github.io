# Restaurants

---

## Bamboo’s Cafe
*Tuktuk Siadong · Café, Indonesian*
One of the nicer spots in Tuktuk for lake views and relaxed dining — tasty local food, though some dishes could use bolder seasoning.
→ [Read the full review](/food/indonesia/sumatra/samosir/restaurants/bamboos-cafe/)

---

## Horas Restaurant
*Tuktuk Siadong · Indonesian, Batak*
Simple restaurant with beautiful lake views and genuinely tasty home-style cooking — the pork saksang is a highlight.
→ [Read the full review](/food/indonesia/sumatra/samosir/restaurants/horas-restaurant/)

---

## Jenny’s Restaurant
*Tuktuk Siadong · Grilled Fish, Indonesian*
The most famous restaurant on Samosir, and deservedly so — the marinated BBQ fish is superb and well worth coming for.
→ [Read the full review](/food/indonesia/sumatra/samosir/restaurants/jennys-restaurant/)

---

## Juwita Cafe
*Tuktuk Siadong · Indonesian, Batak*
One of the most charming eateries in Tuktuk, with an attractive garden terrace overlooking the lake and authentic, nicely prepared local dishes.
→ [Read the full review](/food/indonesia/sumatra/samosir/restaurants/juwita-cafe/)

---

## Maruba Restaurant
*Tuktuk Siadong · Indonesian, Batak*
Family-run restaurant with good homemade Batak cooking — the ayam semur is particularly good, and the fresh avocados from the tree are a lovely touch.
→ [Read the full review](/food/indonesia/sumatra/samosir/restaurants/maruba-restaurant/)
