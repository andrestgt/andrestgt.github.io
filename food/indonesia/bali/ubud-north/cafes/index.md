# Cafés in Ubud & the North

Ubud has a thriving café scene, from hipster specialty roasters to old-school warungs that have been serving good coffee for decades.

---

## CAFÉ DES ARTISTES
Ubud · Café & Bistro
One of Ubud's nicest cafés — good Western bistro food and a pleasant atmosphere, though prices are on the higher side.
→ [Read the full review](./cafe-des-artistes.html)

## GANGGA COFFEE UBUD
Peliatan · Specialty Coffee
Possibly the best café in town — modern, bright, comfortable, with excellently prepared coffees at 30K. Not the best location, but absolutely recommended.
→ [Read the full review](./gangga-coffee.html)

## KAFE
Ubud · Café
A Ubud institution for good reason — strong, well-prepared coffees in generous mugs at great value, with Wi-Fi and comfortable seats.
→ [Read the full review](./kafe.html)

## MINGLE
Ubud · Café
A comfy corner café with decent, inexpensive coffee — fine for a quick stop, though there are better options in town.
→ [Read the full review](./mingle.html)

## MONSIEUR SPOON UBUD
Ubud · Café & Patisserie
A chic café-patisserie with good croissants, decent baguettes, and solid coffee — among the pricier options in Ubud.
→ [Read the full review](./monsieur-spoon.html)

## MOTHER
Mas · Café
A well-prepared flat white at 28K++ in a pleasant setting — simple, unpretentious, and worth knowing about.
→ [Read the full review](./mother.html)

## SENIMAN COFFEE
Ubud · Specialty Coffee
Ubud's most talked-about hipster café — the coffee really is among the best in town, though the decor is an acquired taste and prices reflect the reputation.
→ [Read the full review](./seniman-coffee.html)

## USHA CAFE & BAKERY
Mas · Café & Bakery
A smooth, well-prepared cappuccino in a mug at 30K, served with a bottle of cold water — worth a stop for the coffee and the baked goods.
→ [Read the full review](./usha-cafe-bakery.html)
