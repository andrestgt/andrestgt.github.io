# Restaurants

Ubud has one of the best and most varied restaurant scenes in Bali, from fine dining above the river gorge to simple warungs serving exceptional nasi campur.

---

## Bebek Bengil Ubud
*Ubud · Indonesian, Duck*
The famous roast duck place with magical rice paddy views — the setting is spectacular, but the duck itself is drier than expected and the prices hard to justify.
→ [Read the full review](/food/indonesia/bali/ubud-north/restaurants/bebek-bengil/)

---

## Bollero Resto & Bar
*Ubud · Indonesian*
Years of competition haven't dented this place — the ikan pepes is fantastic, prices are reasonable, and it remains firmly recommended.
→ [Read the full review](/food/indonesia/bali/ubud-north/restaurants/bollero-resto/)

---

## Bridges Bali Restaurant Ubud
*Sayan · Fine Dining*
The best fine dining restaurant in Ubud — a stunning riverside setting, impeccable service, and mouth-watering food at prices that won't break the bank by Bali fine dining standards.
→ [Read the full review](/food/indonesia/bali/ubud-north/restaurants/bridges-bali/)

---

## Honeymoon Kitchen
*Ubud · Indonesian*
A beautifully designed place for a small meal — lovely nasi campur in a gorgeous setting, though drinks are on the pricier side.
→ [Read the full review](/food/indonesia/bali/ubud-north/restaurants/honeymoon-kitchen/)

---

## Lala & Lili Warung
*Sayan · Indonesian*
A blissfully quiet warung above town, away from the traffic, with tasty nasi campur and very inexpensive drinks.
→ [Read the full review](/food/indonesia/bali/ubud-north/restaurants/lala-and-lili/)

---

## Ryoshi Japanese Restaurant Ubud
*Ubud · Japanese*
Once Ubud's best Japanese address, now seriously diminished — poor service, listless presentation, and prices that have risen while quality has fallen.
→ [Read the full review](/food/indonesia/bali/ubud-north/restaurants/ryoshi/)

---

## Taco Casa Fresh Mexican Grill & Bar
*Mas · Mexican*
The best Tex-Mex in South East Asia — huge portions, consistent quality, excellent value, and a horrible location on the main road (get a takeaway).
→ [Read the full review](/food/indonesia/bali/ubud-north/restaurants/taco-casa/)

---

## Tutmak Warung Kopi
*Ubud · Indonesian*
Still going after all these years — the tuna spring rolls remain a favourite and prices are still reasonable, even if the coffee has been overtaken by newer places.
→ [Read the full review](/food/indonesia/bali/ubud-north/restaurants/tutmak/)

---

## Warung Mendez
*Penestanan · Indonesian*
A very good choice in Penestanan, especially for the gorgeous BBQ goat leg with yellow rice — generous portions at net prices.
→ [Read the full review](/food/indonesia/bali/ubud-north/restaurants/warung-mendez/)

---

## Warung Pulau Kelapa
*Kedewatan · Indonesian*
A former favourite that has priced itself out — still decent cooking in a beautifully designed room, but prices have risen sharply while quality hasn't kept pace.
→ [Read the full review](/food/indonesia/bali/ubud-north/restaurants/warung-pulau-kelapa/)

---

## Warung Rama
*Mas · Indonesian*
One of the best nasi campur in Ubud for 30K — tiny place, tiny menu, but the food is freshly prepared and excellent.
→ [Read the full review](/food/indonesia/bali/ubud-north/restaurants/warung-rama/)

---

**Price level note:**
Price levels refer to the approximate cost for **two people** ordering an average meal **without alcoholic drinks**.
