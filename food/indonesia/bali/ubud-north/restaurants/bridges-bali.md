Bridges is my favourite fine dining restaurant in Ubud. I had a nice meal at Mozaic but I prefer Bridges for its affordability and atmosphere. The food is less refined and complicated, yet absolutely mouth-wateringly delicious, with foie gras the star of the show. The whole setting is stunning and the service impeccable, yet its prices are not as over the top as some other of Bali's luxury dining establishments.
