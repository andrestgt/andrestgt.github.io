It's all getting a bit scene-y as the day turns into night, but as far as beach clubs go, Ku De Ta is of course legendary. The food is great, and while drinking here will make you poor, prices for their high-quality grub are actually very reasonable. The menu is international, but leaning towards Japanese. Fantastic unagi is served here.
