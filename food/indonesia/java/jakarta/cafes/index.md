# Cafés

---

## Häagen-Dazs
*Tanah Abang · Café, Ice Cream*
Skip the ice cream and come for the surprisingly affordable Illy cappuccino — under 30k in central Jakarta is genuinely good value.
→ [Read the full review](/food/indonesia/java/jakarta/cafes/haagen-dazs/)

---

## The People’s Cafe
*Grand Indonesia · Café*
Comfortable and popular, with surprisingly well-prepared cappuccinos and good snacks at reasonable prices.
→ [Read the full review](/food/indonesia/java/jakarta/cafes/the-peoples-cafe/)

---

## Segafredo
*Plaza Indonesia · Espresso Bar*
Very well-prepared coffee with an excellent roast profile — slightly steep prices, but considerably better value than the Starbucks nearby.
→ [Read the full review](/food/indonesia/java/jakarta/cafes/segafredo/)

---

## Tanamera Coffee
*Tanah Abang · Specialty Coffee*
One of Jakarta’s best coffee shops, with an intense and excellent roast profile — the lack of comfortable seating is the only real drawback.
→ [Read the full review](/food/indonesia/java/jakarta/cafes/tanamera-coffee/)
