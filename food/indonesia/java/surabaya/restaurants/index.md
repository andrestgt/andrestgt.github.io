# Restaurants in Surabaya

A selection of restaurants in Surabaya, from East Javanese canteen cooking to Japanese and Chinese dim sum.

---

**Price level note:**
Price levels refer to the approximate cost for **two people** ordering an average meal **without alcoholic drinks**.

---

## IMPERIAL KITCHEN & DIMSUM – PAKUWON MALL
Pakuwon Mall · Chinese, Dim Sum
Very good dim sum in a nicer setting than the Tunjungan branch, with better service and consistent quality throughout.
→ [Read the full review](./imperial-kitchen-pakuwon.html)

## IMPERIAL KITCHEN & DIMSUM – TUNJUNGAN PLAZA
Tunjungan Plaza 4 · Chinese, Dim Sum
Really good dim sum, though the setting and service at this branch are noticeably weaker than at Pakuwon.
→ [Read the full review](./imperial-kitchen-tunjungan.html)

## RUMAH MAKAN LAKSANA JAYA II
Kota Lama · East Javanese
Old-school canteen-style eatery in the Old Town, serving excellent sop buntut and proper Javanese satay.
→ [Read the full review](./rumah-makan-laksana-jaya.html)

## SUSHI TEI PAKUWON MALL
Pakuwon Mall · Japanese
Consistent Sushi Tei quality in a mall setting — the food is excellent, though the atmosphere is the weaker of the two branches.
→ [Read the full review](./sushi-tei-pakuwon.html)

## SUSHI TEI TP
Tunjungan Plaza · Japanese
The original Surabaya Sushi Tei, with city views and the edge in atmosphere over the Pakuwon branch.
→ [Read the full review](./sushi-tei-tp.html)
