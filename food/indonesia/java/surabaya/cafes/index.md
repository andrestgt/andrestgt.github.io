# Cafés in Surabaya

A selection of cafés across Surabaya, from specialty coffee in the Old Town to colonial-era buildings and Instagram-friendly spots in the west of the city.

---

## COFFEE TOFFEE APSARI GRAHADI
Genteng · Café
Set in a beautiful old colonial building near the Alun-Alun — the location is the real draw, and prices are very reasonable.
→ [Read the full review](./coffee-toffee-apsari.html)

## DEWATA & MONSIEUR SPOON PRÉFÉRÉ
Comunale West Market · Café & Patisserie
An over-the-top Parisian setup right opposite Pakuwon Mall — slightly ridiculous, but fun, with good cheesecakes and decent coffee.
→ [Read the full review](./dewata-monsieur-spoon.html)

## FIFTEENTH COFFEE KOTA LAMA
Krembangan · Café
A nicely designed café with a rooftop terrace in the Old Town, with well-made coffee at fair prices — though the 3 pm opening is a real limitation.
→ [Read the full review](./fifteenth-coffee.html)

## LE PETIT JEMMA
Juanda Airport · Café
A surprisingly good and inexpensive flat white for an airport café — just don't expect a proper mug.
→ [Read the full review](./le-petit-jemma.html)

## REDBACK SPECIALTY COFFEE
Dukuhpakis · Specialty Coffee
A well-known café in West Surabaya with good service, though starting to show its age and on the pricier side for what it offers.
→ [Read the full review](./redback-specialty-coffee.html)

## THE LIBRARY COFFEE & PASTRIES
Wiyung · Book Café
A pleasant book café inside the Waterplace complex — mornings are quiet and relaxed, coffee is decent if slightly overpriced for Surabaya.
→ [Read the full review](./the-library.html)

## ZERØGRAM KOTA LAMA
Krembangan · Specialty Coffee
One of the few proper coffee spots in Kota Lama, with well-made cappuccinos under 30K — basic setting, but worth it for the coffee.
→ [Read the full review](./zerogam-kota-lama.html)
