# Restaurants in Porto


## CHURRASQUEIRA MOURA
Porto · Restaurant
Cafeteria-style local restaurant — not winning awards for atmosphere, but the grilled meat with their homemade spicy sauce is genuinely very good. Prices are low.
→ [Read the full review](./churrasqueira-moura/)

## MARISCARIA BOM SUCESSO
Porto · Seafood Restaurant
Fantastic place for fresh seafood at some of the best prices you'll find anywhere in Europe. The lunch set costs 6,90€ including wine, while seafood tapas, clams, and barnacles are often under 4€.
→ [Read the full review](./mariscaria-bom-sucesso/)

## PISCA
Porto · Wine Bar & Tapas
Attractive little tapas and wine bar overlooking the Douro, with vintage trams passing by outside the veranda. The food and wine are both good.
→ [Read the full review](./pisca/)

## RESTAURANTE PRAIA DA LUZ
Porto · Restaurant
Rather fancy restaurant by the beach with excellent sea views and very comfortable surroundings. Prices are extremely high by local standards.
→ [Read the full review](./restaurante-praia-da-luz/)
