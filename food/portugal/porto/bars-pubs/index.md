# Bars & Pubs in Porto


## PISCA
Porto · Wine Bar
Attractive little tapas and wine bar overlooking the Douro, with vintage trams passing by outside the veranda. The food and wine are both good. Perhaps a little pricey.

## BEIRA DOURO
Vila Nova de Gaia · Bar
Touristy bar-restaurant with excellent views over Porto. A litre jug of sangria costs 7€ — a perfectly acceptable stop for the combination of price and location.
