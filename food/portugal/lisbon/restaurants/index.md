# Restaurants in Lisbon


## SUPER MÁRIO
Lisbon · Restaurant
Initially looks like a tourist trap but still feels rooted in its working-class origins. Simple, genuinely tasty food at very fair prices — a large jug of vinho verde with seafood rice comes to around 20€.
→ [Read the full review](./super-mario/)

## TASCARDOSO
Lisbon · Restaurant
Small local eatery serving huge portions of simple, tasty food at local prices, usually around 6–8€ for a main. The house white wine is good as well.
→ [Read the full review](./tascardoso/)

## O PRADO
Lisbon · Restaurant
The portions are so huge they could practically roll you out afterwards. The food is good, prices are reasonable, and the snails are especially worth ordering.
→ [Read the full review](./o-prado/)

## RESTAURANTE IDEAL
Lisbon · Restaurant
Simple local eatery with cheap wine and reasonably friendly service. Grilled fish costs well under 10€. A very good option if you're trying to eat well on a budget.
→ [Read the full review](./restaurante-ideal/)

## EATING BEAR TABERNA LUSITANA
Lisbon · Restaurant
Excellent wine and petiscos with a slight Asian twist. The stuffed pepper with crab cheese was especially good. A more modern alternative to the usual Lisbon tavern.
→ [Read the full review](./eating-bear/)

## MARUTO
Lisbon · Bistro
Cute little bistro serving good tibornas and other small dishes. Stick with wine or craft beer rather than the cocktails.
→ [Read the full review](./maruto/)

## TABERNA DA SAUDADE
Lisbon · Restaurant
Tiny and cute, but largely aimed at tourists. The food is good and prices fairly reasonable, although the drinks should really cost less.
→ [Read the full review](./taberna-da-saudade/)

## TIME OUT MARKET
Lisbon · Food Market
A great concept and basically a paradise for foodies. Can get unbelievably crowded, and prices are high by Lisbon standards.
→ [Read the full review](./time-out-market/)
