Eating Bear serves excellent wine and petiscos with a slight Asian twist. The stuffed pepper with crab cheese was especially good. It's a nice option if you want something a little more modern and different from the usual Lisbon tavern food. The only slight disappointment was the couvert, which feels fairly ordinary for the price.
