It's a sad prospect to think that Bangkok's street food scene is an endangered species with government plans to move it all inside. If the new breed of street food courts were all like SOOKSIAM, the idea would be much easier to swallow though.

This place is absolutely stunning, and you get pretty much all your favourite street food meals here, at prices that seem incredibly low given the luxurious surroundings and decorations. Food quality is high, which it obviously has to be if they want to survive in Bangkok. But SOOKSIAM is more than just a place to eat — along with ICONSIAM, the mall in which it is located, it's a first-class tourist attraction in its own right, and in my opinion a must visit for all visitors to the city.
