Although similarly infested with Chinese tour groups, I preferred the pre-COVID Ratchada night market to this new location.

With all the visually spectacular food offerings and frenetic atmosphere, it's definitely a great place to visit for first-time visitors to Bangkok, but for locals or Thailand veterans, there's no reason to come here. The food is overpriced and overhyped and the tour groups can be extremely annoying and inconsiderate.
