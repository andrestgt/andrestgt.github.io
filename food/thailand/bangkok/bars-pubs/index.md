# Bars & pubs

---

## Buasaard
*Phra Nakhon · Bar*
A very local bar with live music, good food and drinks, inexpensive by Bangkok standards, with truly massive XXL cocktails.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/bars-pubs/buasaard/)

---

## Isara Local Bar & Brewery
*Khlong San · Microbrewery*
A small microbrewery in an old wooden house behind the Hilton, opposite IconSiam, surprisingly good beers and excellent larb moo tord. A bit pricey but a very enjoyable evening.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/bars-pubs/isara-brewery/)

---

## Moonshine Bar
*Phra Nakhon · Bar*
Draft pints at 89 Bt is the real draw; the food, freshly wok-cooked right in front of you, is disappointingly bland.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/bars-pubs/moonshine-bar/)

---

## Octave Rooftop Lounge & Bar
*Watthana · Rooftop Bar*
Surprisingly unpretentious for a 5-star hotel sky bar, great views, good cocktails, and reasonable happy hour prices between 5 and 7 pm.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/bars-pubs/octave-rooftop/)

---

## River Vibe Bar & Restaurant
*Samphanthawong, Talat Noi · Rooftop Bar*
One of the few rooftop bars in Bangkok that doesn't break the bank, great sunset views over the river, properly spicy Thai food, and two dishes with a couple of happy hour beers for around 500 Bt.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/bars-pubs/river-vibe/)

---

## Samkaew Craft Beer Kanlapaphruek
*Thonburi · Craft Beer Bar*
Worth the journey out to Thonburi, buy-one-get-one craft beers at around 150 Bt for two half-litres, live music, and very spicy proper Thai food. A lively evening out.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/bars-pubs/samkaew-craft-beer/)

---

## Tawandang German Brewery
*Yan Nawa · Beer Hall*
Good beer at 140 Bt for a 0.5l Weizen, overpriced food that's nothing special, but an essential Bangkok cultural experience; the huge beer hall atmosphere is unique.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/bars-pubs/tawandang/)

---

## Tilac
*Watthana · Bar*
If you want to experience one go-go bar in Bangkok, make it this one, no rip-offs, friendly service, and cocktails properly mixed at 180 Bt, the same price as a beer.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/bars-pubs/tilac/)
