I doubt there are many places in the world where you can get sushi of decent quality for so little money. The fish tasted fresh throughout, and although the cuts are a little on the thin side, there's not much to complain about when you're paying only 40 to 80 Bt for a plate of two nigiri.

You can either pick dishes from the conveyor belt or order directly from the screen at your table. The whole process is automated and very straightforward. Two thumbs up.

Update: It has become extremely popular. Even outside of peak hours, it's now hard to get a table without waiting at least half an hour.
