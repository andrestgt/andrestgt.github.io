This is a really nice restaurant with khlong view and super friendly service. The food is standard Thai fare for tourist palates but it's fresh, tasty and quite affordable. We had Rangoon crab cakes, massaman curry and chicken with cashew nuts and paid around 400 Bt with beverages. Certainly one of the nicest restaurants in this area.
