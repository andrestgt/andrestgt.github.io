Ranks high on my personal "favourite restaurants in the world" list. Unbeatable value, absolutely delicious fusion food. The foie gras unagi don is out of this world. You can eat here for less than 1000 Bt per table of 4 and still have a proper gourmet experience. I'm really surprised this place is not more renowned. For me, even in a city like Bangkok where you're spoilt for choice, this is one that stands out.
