The bad reviews here are thoroughly undeserved in my opinion. The service was OK and very friendly even though we visited at a busy time. We were greeted with a complimentary scallop brochette and a cold towel. The fish and seafood was fresh and of good quality, the Hokkaido scallops were amazing even, and the foie gras was cooked à point. We ended up paying around 700 Bt for a gourmet meal. There was really nothing to complain about.
