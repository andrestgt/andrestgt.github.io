Out of all the amazing dishes Bangkok has to offer, Pad Thai isn't exactly the one I'd be queuing for, so I'm not sure why this place is so incredibly popular. As it happened, we passed by, saw there's no queue and gave it a try. We went for their special pad thai wrapped inside an omelette. And yes, it's a nice pad thai, a little sweet maybe, but with a couple of fresh crunchy king prawns on top. 150 Bt is not cheap, but it's okay.

So yes, I wouldn't queue here more than two minutes, but I don't regret trying it either.
