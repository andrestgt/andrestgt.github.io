You don't come here for atmosphere, it has a cheap canteen feel to it. But the place has been immensely popular for many years with locals and tourists alike and their menu features a great variety of Thai dishes. Prices are reasonable and the food is very good. I only deduct one star because everything is cooked Bangkok style, meaning with a lot of sugar, while personally I prefer my Thai food a bit less sweet. Cannot fault the quality though. Count on spending 150–200 Bt per head.
