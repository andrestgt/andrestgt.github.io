I usually don't recommend places that offer only paper cups instead of proper cups for their coffee drinks. But in this case it makes sense, since there's a nice terrace with sweeping views over Bangkok just in front of the café, where you can take your drinks, sit down and enjoy the views. The cappuccino here is good, too. 90 Bt.
