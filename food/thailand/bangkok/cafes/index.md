# Cafés

Bangkok has an extraordinary café scene, with hundreds of quality roasters spread across the city, from atmospheric canal-side spots to polished Sukhumvit specialty roasters.

---

## (Not Just) Another Cup
*Bang Rak · Specialty Coffee*
A nice cup in a nice place, but 100+ Bt is simply too expensive to justify.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/not-just-another-cup/)

---

## Air Space
*Lumphini, Pathum Wan · Café*
Comfy and spacious with professionally prepared flat whites at 90+ Bt — a reliable stop in the Ratchadamri area.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/air-space/)

---

## AKART Bistro & Bar
*Sathon · Specialty Coffee*
Excellent cappuccino for under 100 Bt, free drinking water, and nice decor. Recommended.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/akart/)

---

## Anan Cafe
*Phaya Thai · Café*
Solid beans at just 50 Bt a cappuccino, but the preparation was a bit sloppy on the visit reviewed — foam too loose and served too hot.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/anan-cafe/)

---

## Art Cafe & Bistro
*Phra Nakhon · Café*
Very cozy and comfy little café with strong, well-prepared cappuccino at a very reasonable price. A great find in this area.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/art-cafe/)

---

## Artis Coffee Bangkok
*Khlong Toei · Specialty Coffee*
Very good espresso drinks at 110 Bt — expensive but expected for this location. Nice veranda outside if you can take the heat and noise.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/artis-coffee/)

---

## Bake Brothers
*Khlong Toei Nuea · Café*
Spacious café in Terminal 21 with a pretty good cappuccino at 75 Bt.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/bake-brothers/)

---

## Black Canyon – Ratchadaphisek
*Din Daeng · Café*
Quiet, comfy and spacious — the usual reliable BCC franchise quality, with a decent Pad Thai to match.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/black-canyon-ratchada/)

---

## Black Canyon – Robinson Bang Rak
*Bang Rak · Café*
The least appealing Black Canyon in terms of atmosphere — and beware of cinnamon on the cappuccino.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/black-canyon-bang-rak/)

---

## Brainwake Café
*Watthana · Café*
Nice place, good coffee and authentic noodle soups at fair prices — just watch out for the cinnamon on the cappuccino.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/brainwake/)

---

## bridge art space
*Sathon · Café & Gallery*
Good beans, well-prepared espresso drinks at just 50–60 Bt, and a friendly atmosphere — a very good option in this part of Bangkok.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/bridge-art-space/)

---

## Café Amazon
*Bang Rak · Café*
Pretty bad even for an Amazon — small, hardly any seats, mediocre coffee, and even in-house drinks come in paper cups.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/cafe-amazon/)

---

## Chan Khanom Wan
*Samphanthawong, Chinatown · Café & Dessert*
A beautifully decorated old-fashioned dessert café opposite the Chinatown gate, with a smooth cappuccino at 69 Bt — one of the best-value cups in the area.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/chan-khanom-wan/)

---

## Coffee Beans By Dao – Central Embassy
*Pathum Wan · Café & Bistro*
Stunning outlet with super comfortable armchairs — the coffee is good but not worth 100+ Bt, and the bistro food is pricey but good.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/coffee-beans-by-dao/)

---

## THE COFFEE CLUB – Mode Sathorn
*Bang Rak · Café*
One of the few cafés in Bangkok that opens very early, with very good coffee — but 130+ Bt for a flat white is hard to justify, even with comfy seats and fast WiFi.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/coffee-club-sathorn/)

---

## CPS Coffee
*Khlong Toei Nuea, Watthana · Specialty Coffee*
A welcome alternative to Starbucks at Terminal 21 — very good coffee, but 120 Bt for a cappuccino without table service and uncomfortable seating is a stretch.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/cps-coffee/)

---

## D'ARK
*Watthana · Specialty Coffee*
Very expensive, but probably the best cappuccino in Bangkok at 115 Bt — smooth and strong at the same time, in a super attractive setting.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/dark/)

---

## Dean & Deluca
*Bang Rak · Café & Deli*
Good coffees and cheesecakes in a fancy setting — the Cube branch with upstairs seating is the best in Bangkok.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/dean-and-deluca/)

---

## Doi Chaang Cafe
*Phra Khanong · Café*
The best value café in Gateway Ekamai — a perfectly prepared cappuccino for just 75 Bt.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/doi-chaang/)

---

## ES/PE/RO
*Sathon · Specialty Coffee*
Cute little café opposite the Goethe Institute with excellent flat whites at just 70 Bt — very good value for this neighbourhood.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/espero/)

---

## Happy Espresso
*Pom Prap · Café*
Not comfy, but excellent coffee at 90 Bt with AC and fast WiFi — one of the better cups in town.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/happy-espresso/)

---

## haus89 Samyan
*Bang Rak · Specialty Coffee*
Chic and modern, with well-prepared cappuccinos at 90 Bt — just ask for a proper mug instead of the default paper cup.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/haus89/)

---

## Jaywalk Cafe
*Phra Nakhon · Café*
Tiny and basic, but a very well-prepared cappuccino at 70 Bt in an area not blessed with good coffee options.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/jaywalk/)

---

## KAFI Sathon
*Khlong San · Café*
Nicely decorated and comfortable, with a well-prepared flat white at just 60 Bt — very good value for this part of Bangkok.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/kafi-sathon/)

---

## Kaze Coffee
*Bang Rak · Café*
A well-prepared latte at 85 Bt — one of the many quality small cafés Bangkok's coffee craze has produced over the past 15 years.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/kaze-coffee/)

---

## Mezzo
*Din Daeng · Café*
A Starbucks clone in taste and look, but much cheaper at 70 Bt for a cappuccino and with comfy seats available.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/mezzo/)

---

## Mitr Roast x Brewsters
*Phaya Thai · Specialty Coffee*
A minimalist café with just a few seats, serving excellent coffee at 80 Bt — a great option for quality and value.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/mitr-roast/)

---

## Mother Roaster Talat Noi
*Samphanthawong, Talat Noi · Café*
One of the most photographed café exteriors in Bangkok — worth visiting at least once for the atmosphere, though the coffee at 100 Bt is overpriced for what it is.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/mother-roaster/)

---

## Ninive – Wood-Fired Pizza
*Phra Khanong · Café & Pizzeria*
The pizza place of choice around Phra Khanong — perfectly delicious wood-fired pizza at reasonable prices, best washed down with an ice-cold Beer Lao.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/ninive/)

---

## NOB café
*Bang Rak · Specialty Coffee*
Friendly service and reasonable prices at 85 Bt — though the medium roast was bitter and unsubtle. The other roast may be better.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/nob-cafe/)

---

## Petit Peyton Travelogue Cafe
*Phra Nakhon · Café*
A beautifully decorated vintage café with bookshelves and grand old armchairs — nicely prepared coffees and the best coffee shop in this part of the old city.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/petit-peyton/)

---

## Pobnar Specialty Coffee & Roastery
*Bang Rak · Specialty Coffee*
A slightly hidden café in a narrow Silom soi — one of the best flat whites I had in Bangkok, at under 100 Bt. Worth seeking out.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/pobnar/)

---

## Remedys
*Samphanthawong, Chinatown · Café*
A tranquil oasis in the bustle of Chinatown — very comfy, less expensive than it looks, with a very nice latte at 85 Bt and some craft beer too.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/remedys/)

---

## Richdee's Eatery and Cafe
*Phra Nakhon · Café*
A nicely located canal-side café in Banglamphu with a latte at 60 Bt — a pleasant place to take a break while exploring the area.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/richdees/)

---

## Sané Phuthong
*Pom Prap · Café*
Possibly the most beautiful café in Bangkok — old-world charm, a teak veranda with views of the canal, the old bridge and the Golden Mount, and very good lattes at 80 Bt. A must visit.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/sane-phuthong/)

---

## Segafredo
*Bangkok · Café*
The usual reliably excellent Segafredo espresso at a comparatively reasonable price, with comfy seating available.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/segafredo-branch/)

---

## Segafredo Zanetti Espresso – Rama 9
*Huai Khwang · Café*
One of the better Segafredo outlets — smooth, expertly prepared coffee under 100 Bt, no cinnamon, and comfy seats. A reliable Bangkok favourite.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/segafredo-rama-9/)

---

## Southern Coffee by Pakin
*Pathum Wan · Café*
Very comfy chairs, but horrible coffee served way too hot and overpriced. Avoid.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/southern-coffee/)

---

## Time's Coffee
*Bang Rak · Specialty Coffee*
A tiny shop with just a few tables — not for lingering, but the espresso drinks are excellent at 70 Bt. Great value.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/times-coffee/)

---

## Uni Coffee
*Bangkok · Café*
Paper cups only, but they make sense here — there's a terrace with sweeping views over Bangkok where you can enjoy a decent 90 Bt cappuccino.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/uni-coffee/)

---

## Vespa Cafe Coffee Roaster
*Phra Nakhon · Specialty Coffee*
A quirky soi café with very good espresso drinks at just 50 Bt — always order without cinnamon.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/bangkok/cafes/vespa-cafe/)
