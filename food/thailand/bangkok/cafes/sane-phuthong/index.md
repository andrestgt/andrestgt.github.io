I've been to Bangkok many times and been to many cafés but this is probably the most beautiful of all of them. Old world charm with wooden furniture and a view on the canal, the old bridge and the Golden Mount temple. There's a teak wood veranda, Thai cushions and free ice water. On top of that, they do very good lattes and cappuccinos and prices are affordable (80 Bt a cup). A must visit!
