# Restaurants

Covering Chiang Rai city, Chiang Saen, and Mae Salong.

---

## Imm Pochana
*Mae Salong · Yunnan Chinese*
Sumptuous pork knuckle stew, fresh forest mushrooms, and Yunnan-style fried noodles — big portions, reasonably priced, with the kind of chaotic service that's very much part of the experience.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/chiang-rai/restaurants/imm-pochana/)

---

## Jiraporn Shop
*Chiang Rai · Thai*
Delicious local food in a beautiful non-touristy spot by the river out of town — a whole fried fish for just 150 baht.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/chiang-rai/restaurants/jiraporn-shop/)

---

## Kiao Siang Hai Restaurant
*Chiang Saen · Chinese*
An unexpected find in this sleepy riverside town — homemade Shanghai-style dumplings and Chinese-style larb muu in a ramshackle open-air setting. Not cheap, but recommended.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/chiang-rai/restaurants/kiao-siang-hai/)
