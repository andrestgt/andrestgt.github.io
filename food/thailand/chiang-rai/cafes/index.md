# Cafés

Covering Chiang Rai city, Mae Chan, Mae Salong, and Mae Suai.

---

## Charin Garden Resort
*Mae Suai · Café*
The coffee is weak, but the cakes and pies are really delicious and well priced — worth a stop for the baked goods alone.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/chiang-rai/cafes/charin-garden-resort/)

---

## Choui Fong Tea
*Mae Chan · Tea Café*
Very scenic tea plantations that get overrun by tourists in high season — still worth coming for the café, which serves very instagrammable and tasty tea-based drinks and cakes.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/chiang-rai/cafes/chuoi-fong/)

---

## Melt in Your Mouth
*Chiang Rai · Café*
A very attractive riverside café with good quality food and desserts and a very good cappuccino — prices are on the higher side for Chiang Rai, but still reasonable.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/chiang-rai/cafes/melt-in-your-mouth/)

---

## Sweet Maesalong Cafe
*Mae Salong · Café*
Beautiful views from the mountainside terrace and quality coffees at 65 Bt — a must pit stop when in the Mae Salong area.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/chiang-rai/cafes/sweet-maesalong/)

---

## The Roast Chiang Rai Coffee
*Chiang Rai · Café*
A very good cappuccino at just 50 baht — small place with a sofa and armchairs, good value and a solid cup.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/chiang-rai/cafes/the-roast-chiang-rai/)
