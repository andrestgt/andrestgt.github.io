# Cafés

Covering Chiang Rai city, Mae Chan, Mae Salong, and Mae Suai.

---

## Charin Garden Resort
*Mae Suai · Café*
Great cakes and pies at surprisingly inexpensive prices, mediocre coffee, worth the stop for the desserts alone.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/chiang-rai/cafes/charin-garden-resort/)

---

## Choui Fong Tea
*Chiang Rai · Café*
Beautiful tea plantations that justify the tourist crowds; the café on the grounds serves genuinely tasty tea drinks and cakes that are worth stopping for in their own right.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/chiang-rai/cafes/chuoi-fong/)

---

## Melt in Your Mouth
*Chiang Rai · Café*
A very attractive riverside café with consistently high-quality food and a good cappuccino, slightly expensive by Chiang Rai standards, but the setting makes it feel reasonable.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/chiang-rai/cafes/melt-in-your-mouth/)

---

## Sweet Maesalong Cafe
*Mae Salong · Café*
Excellent coffee on a beautiful mountainside terrace with sweeping views over the hills at 65 Bt a cappuccino; an essential stop if you're passing through Mae Salong.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/chiang-rai/cafes/sweet-maesalong/)
