# Restaurants

A selection of restaurants across Koh Samui, mostly concentrated along the Mae Nam and Bo Put coastline in the north of the island.

---

## The Fish Restaurant
*Mae Nam · Thai, Seafood*
A modest ring road location that belies very good Thai food — beautifully presented and entirely delicious. The steamed seabass is a must.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/koh-samui/restaurants/the-fish-restaurant/)

---

## La Villa Luna Restaurant
*Mae Nam · Italian*
Wood-fired thin-crust pizzas with high quality toppings — not cheap, but a nice place with good happy hour cocktails.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/koh-samui/restaurants/la-villa-luna/)

---

## Little Paradise
*Mae Nam · Thai, Seafood*
A magical spot on a ridiculously picturesque empty beach — a throwback to a different era, with good shakes and delicious fresh coconut.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/koh-samui/restaurants/little-paradise/)

---

## Lucky Elephant
*Maret · Thai*
A noisy ring road location that compensates with good food at reasonable Samui prices — the pad Thai goong comes with 6 large prawns at 130 Bt.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/koh-samui/restaurants/lucky-elephant/)

---

## The Pirates Burger & More
*Mae Nam · International, Thai*
A welcoming little tourist and expat restaurant — very friendly, with decent burgers and Thai food, though portions can be on the small side for the price.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/koh-samui/restaurants/pirates-burger/)

---

## Red Moon
*Bo Put, Fisherman Village · Thai, International*
The best value seaview option in Fisherman Village — on the "wrong" side of the street but with nicely presented food, two-course set menus at 290 Bt, and cocktails at 100 Bt.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/koh-samui/restaurants/red-moon/)

---

## Siriphoca Mr Adul
*Mae Nam · Thai, Seafood*
A big simple beach shack that feels like the Samui of the 90s — solid Thai food with generous prawn portions at rock-bottom prices. The setting and value make it a five-star experience.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/koh-samui/restaurants/siriphoca-mr-adul/)

---

**Price level note:**
Price levels refer to the approximate cost for **two people** ordering an average meal **without alcoholic drinks**.
