★★★★☆ · 15 Jun 2019 · —

4169, Tambon Maret, Ko Samui District, Surat Thani 84310, Thailand

A general rule of thumb in most beach resort towns or islands is that restaurants in less prime locations, away from the beach, have to work harder and offer better value than beach establishments. So they tend to be preferable if you value quality and value over atmosphere.

Lucky Elephant fits this rule quite well, it makes up for its noisy ring road location with good food at reasonable prices (by Samui standards). Pad Thai Goong is 130 Bt, but there's 6 large prawns on the plate
