With most of the seaview restaurants in Fisherman Village charging pretty outrageous prices these days, this friendly place on the "wrong" side of the street comes as a nice surprise. It's far from cheap either but with its nice decor and high food quality, the prices here are justified. Red Moon offers 2 course set menus for 290 Bt. All dishes we tried were nicely presented, generous and tasty. Thai food for foreign palates, yes, but without sacrificing the quality. All cocktails with quality booze cost only 100 Bt when we visited which is a great deal by Samui standards.
