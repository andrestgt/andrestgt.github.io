This is not only a very nicely decorated and friendly café, they also serve the best flat white I've so far encountered on the island, it's rather expensive though, for 95 Baht. The food menu looks very interesting and varied and I'd love to give it a try another time. The croissant I had for breakfast was good, rather than excellent, so it's four stars for now, but I'm definitely coming back.
