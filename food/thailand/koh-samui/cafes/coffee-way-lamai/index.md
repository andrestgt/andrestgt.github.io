A decade ago, I would have been very happy to find coffee of this quality on the island. However, in 2019 a slightly too hot Boncafe flat white is nothing to get excited about anymore. But the place is friendly, air conditioned, and 100 Baht for a coffee and a pretty good croissant is not bad.
