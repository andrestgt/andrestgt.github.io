# Cafés

---

## Coffee Way Lamai
*Lamai Beach · Café*
Friendly, air-conditioned café with decent coffee — a perfectly fine stop, though the flat white is served a bit too hot and the quality is no longer exciting by today's standards.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/koh-samui/cafes/coffee-way-lamai/)

---

## Sasatorn
*Bo Put · Café*
The best flat white on the island — nicely decorated, friendly, and worth a return visit for the food menu as well.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/koh-samui/cafes/sasatorn/)

## The Garage Life
*Mae Nam · Café*
A quirky café with nicely presented beverages and a flat white at just 70 Bt — less expensive than it looks.
