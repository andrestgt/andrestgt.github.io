I came across this place near the pier after seeing a sign for craft beer and Neapolitan pizza, which was enough for me to stop. They were out of craft beer when I visited, apparently after a party the night before, but the wood-fired pizzas more than made up for it. I went for the larb moo pizza, my usual go-to in Thailand, spicy and very satisfying. At under 300 baht, prices are very reasonable, and it's a great little stop if you're exploring the area by bike.
