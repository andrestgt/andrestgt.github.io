I have a suspicion that places like Baristro Asian Style are built mainly with the Instagram and influencer crowd in mind. Even so, it's hard not to be impressed by this one. The entire place — the architecture, the garden, the layout — feels as if you've been transported straight to Japan. It's a coffee and tea lounge that genuinely creates a sense of calm and serenity, and it's a real pleasure to be there, at least when it's not busy or overrun by people taking selfies.

We were lucky on the day we visited and found it relatively quiet, which made the experience much more enjoyable. What ultimately matters, though, is that the coffee is good. A nicely made cappuccino for 85 baht, enjoyed in such beautiful and peaceful surroundings, makes this a worthwhile trip out of town.
