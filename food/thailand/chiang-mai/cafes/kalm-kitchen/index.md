Kalm is a very attractive art and craft centre, and their café is nicely decorated. It's a pleasure to sit here and have a coffee.



The coffee beans they use tend to be on the bitter side though, the ones you usually get in cheap coffee places. Their lattes are well prepared, but for the bean quality, they're overpriced here for 85 Bt.
