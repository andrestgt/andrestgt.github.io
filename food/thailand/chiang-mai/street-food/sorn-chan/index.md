Basic local eatery in a prime location, just in front of Thapae Gate. They have a very limited menu of specifically local versions of Chiang Mai specialties such as khao soi and larb, that taste slightly differently than elsewhere. The larb here was pure minced meat including innards, and the khao soi was slightly sweet. An acquired taste, but not bad at all.
