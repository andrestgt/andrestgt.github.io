Old school Thai eatery that's been around forever serving very good Northern Thai food to tourists and locals alike. It seems to have fallen out of favour a little though since we were the only guests when we visited; it was mostly empty every time we passed by (or maybe it had to do with it being an open air place and the temperatures being above 40°C).
nd yet, as someone who is not the biggest fan of Northern Thai food, I really enjoyed all dishes we ordered here. The pork khao soi, the larb moo, and most of all the spicy sour sausage salad. It's a bit pricier than similar, more local eateries, but still reasonable.
