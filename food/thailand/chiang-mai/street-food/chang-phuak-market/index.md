Small night market, mostly for tourists. The food here is generally good to great. The khao kha moo is one of the best I've ever had. The Esarn food stall at the end of the market has great larb and mango salad. We also got a nice tom yum from the eatery where two old ladies work the wok. If you're looking for an authentic local experience, look elsewhere, but there's some great, inexpensive food here.
