I used to stay around the corner from this place for a month in 2008 and it was my go-to place whenever I felt like meeting other travellers. Fast forward to 2024, new owner, but the look and vibes are exactly the same — it's still the most friendly and sociable bar in town with a great mix of people of all ages and walks of life.
