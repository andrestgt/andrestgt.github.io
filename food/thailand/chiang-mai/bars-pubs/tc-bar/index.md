This friendly little hole-in-the-wall is not just an eatery, but very much Chiang Mai's version of a late-night comfort food place. It's the kind of spot where you end your evening with a couple of inexpensive beers and something simple like chicken teriyaki rice.

Places like this exist for exactly those moments before you go home, when all you want is a quick, tasty bite and a cheap last drink.
