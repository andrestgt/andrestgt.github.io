# Bars & pubs

---

## Chill Out Bar
*Si Phum, Old Town · Bar*
The most friendly and sociable bar in town — a great mix of people of all ages and walks of life, exactly as it was when it opened years ago.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/chiang-mai/bars-pubs/chill-out-bar/)

---

## The Globe Bar & Restaurant
*Si Phum · Bar & Restaurant*
An inexplicably empty bar with very friendly staff, a free pool table, draft Tiger at 45 Bt, and a good selection of local craft beer at very reasonable prices.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/chiang-mai/bars-pubs/globe-bar/)

---

## TC Bar & Grill
*Si Phum, Chang Phueak · Bar*
Chiang Mai's version of a late-night comfort food place — cheap beers, inexpensive bites like chicken teriyaki rice, and a friendly hole-in-the-wall atmosphere.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/chiang-mai/bars-pubs/tc-bar/)

---

## Thai 1 On Bar & Grill
*Si Phum · Bar & Grill*
Good music, tasty cocktails, decent pub grub covering both Thai and Western food — a friendly hangout that edges ahead of the simpler options nearby for everything except cheap draft beer.
→ [Read the full review](/food/thailand/chiang-mai/bars-pubs/thai-1-on-bar/)
