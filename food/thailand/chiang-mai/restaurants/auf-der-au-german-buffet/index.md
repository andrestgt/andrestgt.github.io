This place is an absolute nightmare for anyone trying to be on a healthy diet. There's just enormous amounts of rich, heavy, meaty and sugary food at an unbeatable price.

The quality of the food ranges from mediocre and bland to succulent and delicious, but to be honest, the two of us only managed to try maybe one fourth of what's on offer and still rolled out of the place almost comatose.

I found the staff here very friendly and the free jugs of water were quickly refilled. The filter coffee which is also included in the price here is better than at some of the hipster roasteries in town.
