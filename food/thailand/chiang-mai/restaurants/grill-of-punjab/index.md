Got a delivery with Food Panda. Veg samosas, garlic naan and palak paneer. It's one of the more moderately priced Indian restaurants in town, but my experience wasn't the best. The samosas were fine, as were the dipping sauces. But the garlic naan I got was a plain naan and quite dry. I received a very generous portion of palak paneer, but the chef must have forgotten to put salt, so I had to use the remaining dipping sauces as seasoning.
