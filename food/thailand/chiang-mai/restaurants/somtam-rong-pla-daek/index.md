It's rare to get mediocre food in a random popular Thai restaurant, but I really wasn't impressed with what they serve here, even though I ordered two of my favourite dishes, duck larb and gai yang. The chicken didn't come straight from the roast, but was kept warm and heated up so it was a little dry inside. That's a pity because the flavours were good.
The duck larb came without even a hint of spiciness, with barely any fresh herbs and without roasted rice powder. The minced duck was nice, but it wasn't what I expected.
Service was good, with friendly English speaking staff.
