The portions are quite small for the price and there's no option of ordering a whole chicken, but quality wise it's very good. The chicken is very juicy and well seasoned. The mango salad was a bit sweet though and lacked pungency.

But for a Nimman location it's a reasonable option to satisfy your chicken cravings.
