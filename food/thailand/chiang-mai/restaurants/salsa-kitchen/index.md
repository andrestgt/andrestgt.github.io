I've spent enough time in South East Asia to know that finding really good Mexican food here is very difficult. That's why I had high hopes after reading the reviews of this place.

But my experience was a bit of a disappointment. I had the chipotle chicken enchiladas, which looked great on the plate and came as a very generous portion.

Instead of the usual sour cream, salsa and guacamole, it came with only the former two, without guac, but two additional dips which tasted more Thai than Mexican. The enchiladas were a bit soggy and the chicken filling a bit bland. Even more bland was the cheese, cheap mozzarella instead of flavourful cheddar. It came with a side of refried beans and pineapples (?).

At the prices they charge, they could at least offer a small dip of guacamole and use proper cheese.

Service on the other hand was friendly, and the refried beans were tasty.
