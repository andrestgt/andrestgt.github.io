I'm not a vegan, but I really enjoyed the food here a lot. The vegan khao soi (55 Bt) was as good as any of the meat versions I've had. And their stir-fried shirataki noodles were delicious.

Prices are similar to regular meat eateries, so no vegan hipster surcharges here. Highly recommended.
