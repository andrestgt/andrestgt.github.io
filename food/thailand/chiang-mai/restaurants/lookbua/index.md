As far as tourist-oriented restaurants in Chiang Mai's Old Town go, this is one of the better options for reasonably authentic and affordable Thai food. I tend to avoid places that mainly cater to tourists, but I stopped here because I was in the mood for a red duck curry, which is surprisingly hard to find at a reasonable price. They had it on the menu, and it was good enough that I ended up coming back a few times.

I also tried several other dishes, and everything fell into the same category: solid, well-prepared Thai standards. The service is good, the presentation is pleasant, and while nothing here is especially outstanding, it's a reliable choice if you're looking for decent Thai food in the Old Town.
