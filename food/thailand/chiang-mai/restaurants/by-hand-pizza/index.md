Superb pizzas from the wood fired oven. I had the one with lamb merguez and capers. The crust was perfect and I'd absolutely recommend this place.

I also think that the prices are very reasonable for the quality you get here.

As a matter of fact, the only criticism I have is overload. I thought they could have done with fewer toppings. There was a very generous amount of merguez and onions on the pizza. And I believe in this case less would be more.

But overall it's a great pizza. The anchovy and capers pizza on my second visit was perfect.
