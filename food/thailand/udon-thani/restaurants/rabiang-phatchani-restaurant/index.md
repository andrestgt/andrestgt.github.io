Wonderful lakeshore restaurant with prices that are slightly higher than at ordinary provincial restaurants, but it's a beautiful place to sit and enjoy the views over the lake at sunset. We had the hors d'oeuvre platter with delicacies such as spring rolls, prawn satay and pork filet in banana leaves, plus some duck larb and yum neua, authentically spicy and delicious.
