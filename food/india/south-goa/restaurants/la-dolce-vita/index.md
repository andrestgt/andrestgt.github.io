I had amazing Italian at Piccola Roma in North Goa, so I wanted to see if there's an equivalent in the south. This place looked attractive with its big wood-fired pizza oven. We ordered a standard Pizza Napoli (380 Rs) and tagliatelle con gamberi (410 Rs). Both were perfectly fine, but not the same quality as Piccola Roma, despite similar prices. Still, if you're in South Goa and crave for some proper Italian food, Dolce Vita is a very good option.
