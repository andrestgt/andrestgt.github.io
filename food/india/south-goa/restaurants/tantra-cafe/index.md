Tantra has a million-dollar location on Patnem Beach, a long cocktail happy hour and pretty good food at decent prices. You do pay for the location but not excessively so. A sunset dinner with golden fried calamari rings, malai kofta, butter naan, seafood balchao, six cocktails and two beers cost us 1500 Rs. The balchao was a little sour (a common occurrence in Goan gravy dishes), the fried calamari were very good, tender with just the right amount of crunchy dough.
