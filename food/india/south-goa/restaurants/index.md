# Restaurants

---

## Vidushi's Cow Corner
*Patnem · Goan, International*
My favourite in Patnem, creative pasta with Goan chourico and squid in dill cream, plus a legendary chicken Kathi roll at 140 Rs.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/south-goa/restaurants/vidushis-cow-corner/)

---

## Pepper's
*Madgaon, Margao · Goan, International*
Probably the best food in Margao, interesting fusion dishes, excellent special chicken, and pork ribs in green masala at 1000 Rs for two.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/south-goa/restaurants/peppers/)

---

## O' Cuzinero
*Chandor · Goan*
Upmarket countryside restaurant where non-touristy location keeps prices very reasonable, spectacular chicken pariwar at 270 Rs.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/south-goa/restaurants/o-cuzinero/)

---

## Dreams of Palolem Beach Resort
*Palolem · Goan*
Friendliest beach hut operator in Palolem, stunning sunset views, reasonable beer prices and competent food at 200–300 Rs.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/south-goa/restaurants/dreams-of-palolem/)

---

## Tantra Cafe Restaurant
*Patnem Beach · Goan*
Million-dollar Patnem location with long happy hour, sunset dinner for two including six cocktails and two beers for 1500 Rs.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/south-goa/restaurants/tantra-cafe/)

---

## Longuinhos Bar, Restaurant & Confectioners
*Madgaon, Margao · Goan*
Margao's most atmospheric restaurant straight out of another era, cold cheap beer, melt-in-the-mouth chili pork at 200–300 Rs.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/south-goa/restaurants/longuinhos-bar-restaurant/)

---

## La Dolce Vita
*Agonda · Italian*
A good option for proper Italian in South Goa, decent wood-fired pizza and tagliatelle, just not quite as good as Piccola Roma in the north.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/south-goa/restaurants/la-dolce-vita/)

---

## Mollu's Classic Multicuisine Restaurant
*Palolem · Goan*
Better food than most beach places and slightly cheaper, excellent veg pulao with paneer and mushrooms.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/south-goa/restaurants/mollus-classic-multicuisine/)

---

## Sabina
*Palolem · Goan, Indian*
Good family-run restaurant away from the beach shacks, perfectly flaky tandoori naan and flavourful mutton xacuti.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/south-goa/restaurants/sabina/)

---

## Om Shanti Beach Stay
*Patnem Beach · Goan*
Good beach shack with Bira White on offer and a very nice calamari chili fry for under 300 Rs.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/south-goa/restaurants/om-shanti-beach-stay/)

---

## Seaview Resort Restaurant
*Patnem Beach · Goan*
Candlelit roadside resort restaurant with excellent malai kofta, slightly disappointing squid quantity for 450 Rs.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/south-goa/restaurants/seaview-resort/)

---

## Boom Shankar Resort
*Palolem · Goan*
Quiet seaview terrace with cheap beer and nice squid balls with wasabi, come for solitude.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/south-goa/restaurants/boom-shankar-resort/)

---

## Holiday Inn Cafe
*Palolem · Goan*
Simple friendly eatery on the promontory, standard quality, very reasonable prices, nice momos.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/south-goa/restaurants/holiday-inn-cafe/)

---

## Tanshikar Spice Farm
*Netravli · Goan*
Excellent spice tour in beautiful surroundings; the lunch buffet included in 550 Rs is a little disappointing but the tour is well worth it.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/south-goa/restaurants/tanshikar-spice-farm/)

---

## Agonda Beach Cafe
*Agonda · Goan*
Perfectly decent beach eatery but neither better value nor better food than the nicer places in prime locations nearby.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/south-goa/restaurants/agonda-beach-cafe/)

---

## Nirvana Cafe
*Patnem Beach · Goan*
Good beach shack but the 610 Rs seafood sizzler is probably the most expensive dish in all our Indian travels.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/south-goa/restaurants/nirvana-cafe/)

**Price level note:**
Price levels refer to the approximate cost for **two people** ordering an average meal **without alcoholic drinks**.
