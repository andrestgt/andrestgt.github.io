This German Bakery is a lot less grand than its namesake in Anjuna, but nevertheless a lovely place to chill out. Staff are very friendly and the traveller food is good and reasonably priced. It has a bit of a Himalayan theme and the Tibetan dishes such as momos and thukpa are nice here, as are the cakes. They have an early happy hour starting at 3pm when you can get potent cocktails for around 100 Rs — obviously a steal.
