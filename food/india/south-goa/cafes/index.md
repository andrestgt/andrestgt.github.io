# Cafés

## German Bakery – Palolem
*Palolem · Café*
Lovely chill-out place with good food, Tibetan momos, excellent cakes and potent happy-hour cocktails from 3pm.

## Bombay Coffee Roasters – Palolem
*Palolem · Café*
Best value espresso in Goa at 90 Rs a cappuccino — preparation quality varies, but still the most reliable option in South Goa.
