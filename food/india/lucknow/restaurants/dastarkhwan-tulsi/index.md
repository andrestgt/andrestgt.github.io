This rather basic place is so popular that you might have to wait for a table even in the afternoon outside of peak times. Dastarkhwaan serves traditional Awadhi food which is incredibly rich, heavy and meat-based. We had chicken masala which was super creamy and luscious, mutton rogan josh (mostly bones but tasty), and galawat kebabs which were really nice with a creamy texture. Prices are moderate.
