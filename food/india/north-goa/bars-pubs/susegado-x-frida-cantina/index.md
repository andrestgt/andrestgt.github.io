This large elegant beer garden and microbrewery is probably one of a kind in Goa. They have six different Goan Susegado beers on tap at reasonably priced 180 Rs a glass. I didn't like all 6 of them, but would highly recommend the refreshing tropical Wit and the flavourful, yet not too heavy, vanilla stout.
