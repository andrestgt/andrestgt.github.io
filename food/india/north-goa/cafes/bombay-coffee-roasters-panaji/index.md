All three Bombay Coffee Roasters branches offer AC, reasonable WiFi and Italian-style espresso drinks at very fair prices (90 Rs for a cappuccino). The quality of the coffee itself is good at all three branches, however, only the Anjuna branch has staff who actually know how to prepare a decent cappuccino — the Panaji branch tends to come without or with very bubbly froth. Nevertheless, they're the best value espresso places in Goa, so I'd still recommend stopping by.
