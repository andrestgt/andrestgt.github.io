# Cafés

---

## Artjuna
*Anjuna · Café*
Flawless café with a very good cappuccino (120 Rs), excellent bread, dips, and a Greek croissant sandwich with generous feta.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/north-goa/cafes/artjuna/)

## German Bakery Anjuna
*Anjuna · Café*
Huge garden space with comfy cushion seating, extravagant décor, craft beers, local food, and rare fast WiFi — a must in Anjuna.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/north-goa/cafes/german-bakery-anjuna/)

## G-Shot Coffee Roastery & Cafe
*Assagao · Specialty Coffee*
Hands down the best espresso drinks in Goa — Melbourne quality in a tranquil garden setting, worth the trip to Assagao.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/north-goa/cafes/g-shot-coffee-roastery/)

## Cafe Hygge
*Fontainhas, Panaji · Café*
Beautifully air-conditioned café in Panaji's prettiest neighbourhood — good cappuccino at 100 Rs, no WiFi.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/north-goa/cafes/cafe-hygge/)

## Bombay Coffee Roasters – Panaji
*Fontainhas, Panaji · Café*
Best value espresso in Goa at 90 Rs — but only the Anjuna branch staff actually know how to make a proper cappuccino.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/north-goa/cafes/bombay-coffee-roasters-panaji/)
