I actually prefer the laid-back, casual atmosphere of the German Bakery to the hip scene of Artjuna, but I can't help but appreciate the quality of food and drinks on offer here. The special breakfast (410 Rs with coffee) looks beautiful and tastes great, the bread is excellent, and so are the dips. The Greek croissant sandwich (270 Rs) has a generous amount of feta on a nice buttery croissant. And the cappuccino here is very good too (120 Rs). Flawless.
