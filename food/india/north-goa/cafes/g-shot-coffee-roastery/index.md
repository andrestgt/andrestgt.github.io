The pretty village of Assagao is where you'll find hands down the best espresso drinks in Goa. Coffee culture in India has made a quantum leap in the last decade, but finding a truly perfect cup of flat white or cappuccino is still an absolute rarity, even in the hipster cafés of the biggest cities. G-Shot, in a nice tranquil garden setting, does brew and prepare coffees of a quality and texture you'd expect in the best cafés of Melbourne or Berlin. They also charge prices similar to those in aforementioned cities — around 250 Rs for a coffee. For the best coffees in Goa, it's definitely worth the trip.
