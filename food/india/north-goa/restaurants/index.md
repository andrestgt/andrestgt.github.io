# Restaurants

---

## Piccola Roma Pizza
*Vagator · Italian*
One of the very rare places in Asia serving truly authentic Roman pizzas at almost unbeatable prices — always go for the Napoli (380 Rs).
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/north-goa/restaurants/piccola-roma-pizza/)

---

## Viva Panjim
*Fontainhas, Panaji · Goan*
Most atmospheric place for Goan dinner in a beautiful old Portuguese home — excellent calamari sizzler and cocktails at very reasonable prices.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/north-goa/restaurants/viva-panjim/)

---

## Ritz Classic – Patto
*Patto Plaza, Panaji · Goan*
Always packed at lunch for their massive and spectacular Goan fish thali at around 200 Rs — probably the best I had.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/north-goa/restaurants/ritz-classic-patto/)

---

## Sussegado
*Calangute · Goan*
Possibly the most atmospheric place to eat in touristy Calangute — impeccable Goan food more subtle and less sour than most.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/north-goa/restaurants/sussegado/)

---

## Vinayak Family Restaurant
*Assagao · Goan, Seafood*
Busy family restaurant specialising in Goan seafood next to a rice paddy — good fresh food at beach prices.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/north-goa/restaurants/vinayak-family-restaurant/)

---

## Ankita Classic
*Amberem · Goan, Seafood*
Remote inland restaurant with fresh and inexpensive seafood — 50–100 Rs cheaper than beach prices for equally satisfying food.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/north-goa/restaurants/ankita-classic-warkhand-road/)

---

## Comida Caseira
*Vagator · Goan*
Friendly restaurant in Vagator serving Goan food with a twist — relaxed atmosphere and reasonable prices.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/north-goa/restaurants/comida-caseira/)

---

## Kokni Kanteen Goa
*Panaji · Goan*
Nice restaurant with good Goan specialties and pleasant ambiance — let down by poor customer service on reservation.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/north-goa/restaurants/kokni-kanteen-goa/)
---

## Bee's Knees Cafe
*Arambol · Restaurant*
Typical cheerful backpacker café done just right — chicken thali and shakshouka both tasty and filling at around 200 Rs.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/north-goa/restaurants/bees-knees-cafe/)
