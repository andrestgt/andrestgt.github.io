A most attractive restaurant in Panaji's colonial district, this place is perfect for an atmospheric Goan dinner. Prices are very reasonable — not just for food, but also for drinks, with cocktails around 200 Rs. A generous calamari sizzler goes for 350 Rs and it's one of the best we had in Goa. The Goan curries are authentic and as in other places I found them a little too heavy on the vinegar, but that's just me. Beautiful, yet casual dining room in an old Portuguese home.
