Staying in the Majestic area, there's not a whole lot of options for family-friendly dining but this place seemed more suitable than other mostly male-dominated places around. It's a very basic old-school eatery with a certain grumpy charm. Prices are rock bottom but the food is pretty good; we even came back twice. Chicken 65 with their fluffy parathas is a good choice. Most non-veg dishes are 150 Rs or less.
