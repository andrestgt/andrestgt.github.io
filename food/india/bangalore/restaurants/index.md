# Restaurants

---

## MTR Restaurant
*Malleshwaram · South Indian*
Bangalore institution for excellent veg South Indian food — the mini thali at 150 Rs is the best value and everything but small.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/bangalore/restaurants/mtr-bangalore/)

---

## Nagarjuna Restaurant
*Residency Road · Andhra*
Traditional Andhra food on banana leaves — full thali with unlimited rice for 200 Rs, very good spicy chicken on the side.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/bangalore/restaurants/nagarjuna-restaurant/)

---

## Hotel New Grand
*Cottonpete · Indian*
Rock-bottom prices with surprisingly good food — the chicken 65 with fluffy parathas is a good choice at under 150 Rs.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/bangalore/restaurants/hotel-new-grand/)
