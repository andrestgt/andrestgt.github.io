During lunch hours it's almost impossible to get a table in this super popular Bangalore institution, so best to come early or just before they close in the afternoon. They have a mini thali for around 150 Rs and a special thali for around 300. The mini thali was everything but small and much better value. The all-veg South Indian food here really is very good and deservedly popular. Friendly, professional service and very tourist-friendly environment.
