We visited three of Bangalore's biggest brew pubs and Biergarten in Koramangala was a clear favourite in terms of service, friendliness, design, and quality of beers and food. We tried around eight craft beers from tap — all were good, some such as the smoked lager were exceptional, taking you straight back to Bamberg. The grub here is great and quite budget-friendly. The meze platter (275 Rs) came with tasty falafel, houmous and babaganoush. Fantastic happy hour from 4–7pm with beers at 40% discount.
