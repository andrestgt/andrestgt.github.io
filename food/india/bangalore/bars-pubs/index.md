# Bars & Pubs

Bangalore has a thriving craft beer scene with several excellent brew pubs.

---

## Biergarten Brewery & Kitchen
*Koramangala · Craft Beer Bar*
The best of Bangalore's brew pubs — exceptional smoked lager, great meze platter, and 40% off beers from 4–7pm.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/bangalore/bars-pubs/biergarten-brewery/)

---

## Brewklyn Microbrewery
*Kalyan Nagar · Craft Beer Bar*
Excellent home brews including Ale Capone, with long happy hours until 7pm — eat and drink well for under 1000 Rs per head.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/bangalore/bars-pubs/brewklyn-microbrewery/)

---

## Arbor Brewing Company
*Ashok Nagar · Craft Beer Bar*
Most centrally located of Bangalore's craft breweries — friendly, well-designed, with the Smooth Criminal and pumpkin ale as highlights.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/bangalore/bars-pubs/arbor-brewing-company/)

---

## Pecos Hideout Koramangala
*Koramangala · Bar*
Decent rock bar with good music and relatively inexpensive beers — not busy midweek but might be livelier on weekends.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/bangalore/bars-pubs/pecos-hideout/)
