Spacious, chic café with very comfortable seats, table service and very reasonable prices. Espresso drinks are served in Starbucks-style mugs, so it's not proper Italian style, but the cappuccino (120 Rs) was good and less expensive than even CCD or Barista. Negatives are the excruciatingly slow WiFi and no sockets.
