## Open Hand Café & Shop Varanasi
*Assi Ghat · Café*
The best coffees in Varanasi — go for a double-shot cappuccino at 110 Rs; comfy hangout with good cheap food, very slow service.

## Mona Lisa Cafe
*Bangali Tola · Café*
Comfy and friendly hangout for masala chai (60 Rs) and veg pakoras (90 Rs) — their homemade chili sauce is super delicious.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/varanasi/cafes/mona-lisa-cafe/)

## Brown Bread Bakery Varanasi
*Bangali Tola · Bakery, Café*
Come for the fantastic bread, not the coffee — the food and drinks leave much to be desired but the bread is the real thing.
