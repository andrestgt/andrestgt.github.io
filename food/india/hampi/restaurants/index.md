# Restaurants

---

## Hotel Hampi International – Steps Restaurant
*Hosapete · Indian*
Unexpectedly excellent restaurant in a station hotel — elegant atmosphere at backpacker prices, with stunningly flavourful chicken noodles and rich Rogan Josh.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/hampi/restaurants/hotel-hampi-international/)

---

## Mango Tree Restaurant
*Hampi · Indian*
Hampi's most popular traveller café with friendly service and very good thalis at 120–200 Rs — avoid the "Lavazza" cappuccino.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/hampi/restaurants/mango-tree-restaurant/)

---

## Chillout Bamboo Restaurant
*Hampi · Indian*
Great place to chill for hours without pressure — comfy and friendly, similar prices and quality to Mango Tree but much less crowded.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/hampi/restaurants/chillout-bamboo-restaurant/)

---

## Ravi's Rose Restaurant
*Hampi · Indian*
Standard rooftop traveller café with comfy seats and sockets — decent aubergine masala, but vendors allowed to pester customers.
→ [Read the full review](/food/india/hampi/restaurants/ravis-rose-restaurant/)
