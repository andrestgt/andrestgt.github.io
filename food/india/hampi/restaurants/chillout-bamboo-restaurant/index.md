Hampi's second most popular restaurant after Mango Tree is a great place to chill out for hours without crowds or pressure to vacate your table. It's comfy and friendly and while the prices are pretty much the same as Mango Tree and the food not quite as good, it's a very pleasant place to spend a lot of time.
