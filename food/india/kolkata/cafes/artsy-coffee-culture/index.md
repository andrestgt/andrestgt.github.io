An oasis hidden away from the chaos and dirt of Calcutta's streets. The café is beautifully furnished with comfy chairs, sofas and bookshelves. Good international bistro food is served along with a good variety of espresso drinks. Prices are reasonable — most dishes under 300 Rs and a flat white for only 140 Rs. It's only a four-star review because I was served the wrong wrap without explanation, and the flat white didn't have the proper silky texture I'd expect from a specialty coffee shop. Good enough, but just missed the mark.
