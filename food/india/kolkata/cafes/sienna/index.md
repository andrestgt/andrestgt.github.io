The friendliest, most attractive café in the Park Street area, hidden on the first floor of a backstreet building. Smiling staff and a nice interior design make you feel so welcome you could happily spend the best part of your day here. Short menu of tasty, well-prepared international bistro food with dishes around 300 Rs. I'd give 5 stars if it weren't for the lack of sockets and the cappuccino which at 180 Rs is pricey and was served too hot.
