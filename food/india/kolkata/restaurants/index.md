*Park Street · Indian*
Deservedly famous and popular, quality North Indian and continental food for two under 1000 Rs, worth the queue.

## The Bhoj Company
*Lal Bazar · Bengali*
Exceptional first experience with Bengali food, fresh chingri malai curry thali and bhetki in mustard oil for under 800 Rs.

## Limelight Restaurant & Bar
*BBD Bagh · Indian*
Lovably unconsciously uncool, time-warp décor, six big king prawns in chingri malai curry, and sumptuous palak paneer.
