# Bars & Pubs

## The Lords and Barons
*Park Street · Bar*
Atmospheric pub with probably the best happy hour in town, 169 Rs for selected drinks Mon–Thu until 8pm, excellent Harissa chicken.

## Olypub
*Park Street · Bar*
A charming time-warp boozer, go for Bira 91 or Simba rather than the draft, and order the legendary beef steak at 250 Rs.
