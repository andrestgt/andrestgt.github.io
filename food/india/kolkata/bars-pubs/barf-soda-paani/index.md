This place caters mainly to rich kids and hipsters wanting to let their hair down and get drunk in a safe atmosphere. Fair enough, but if you're a couple just coming for a few good drinks and a nice meal, this is a bad option. The service is horrible and most items on the menu are unavailable, including basic stuff such as white wine, gin and any beer that's not a boring lager. We ordered 2 dishes, the second of which never arrived. An extra star for the one meal we actually got, spicy sizzling paneer tikka noodles, which was nice.
