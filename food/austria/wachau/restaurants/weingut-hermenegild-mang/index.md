A beautiful winery and Gasthof in a picturesque village in the Wachau. It's a large and well-run place. The house wines are very good and reasonably priced. With your wine, you can order some really nice cheese and sausage platters that are bigger than you would expect for the price. The blood sausage is especially tasty. Don't miss this when you're in the area.
