# Cafés

---

## Feeka Coffee Roasters
*Bukit Bintang, KL · Specialty Coffee*
Lovely café tucked away in the quieter residential side of Bukit Bintang — smooth, properly prepared cappuccino at around 11 RM.
→ [Read the full review](/food/malaysia/kuala-lumpur/cafes/feeka-coffee-roasters/)

## Plan B Roasters Publika
*Publika, KL · Specialty Coffee*
Excellent coffee at fair prices and one of the better café options in Publika — overlooks BIG's absurdly well-stocked supermarket.
→ [Read the full review](/food/malaysia/kuala-lumpur/cafes/plan-b-roasters-publika/)

---

## Nosh&Co
*Jalan Ampang, KL · Café*
Huge and stylish yet much more affordable than it looks — flat whites at 9 RM with an impressively long happy hour.
→ [Read the full review](/food/malaysia/kuala-lumpur/cafes/nosh-and-co/)

---

## Jardin Coffee & Tea
*Berjaya Times Square, KL · Café*
Very decent café stop inside Times Square with fast WiFi, power sockets and a surprisingly good cappuccino for under 12 RM.
→ [Read the full review](/food/malaysia/kuala-lumpur/cafes/jardin-coffee-and-tea/)
