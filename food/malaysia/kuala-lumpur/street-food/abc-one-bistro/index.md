Busy and popular 24-hour fast-food eatery with a menu covering Malay, Indian, Chinese, and even some Western dishes. It can feel a bit chaotic and the staff seem overworked, so service isn't quite as fast as you might expect. That said, it's a solid option in the area. The roti canai was very good, and the dips were excellent.
