# Street Food

---

## Wong Ah Wah Restaurant
*Jalan Alor, KL · Chinese*
The famous chicken wings really are worth the hype — a must stop while wandering along Jalan Alor.
→ [Read the full review](/food/malaysia/kuala-lumpur/street-food/wong-ah-wah-restaurant/)

---

## Warung R&J
*Jalan Alor, KL · Malay*
Local nasi lemak, nasi goreng and char kway teow for well under 10 RM — cheap, tasty and refreshingly unpretentious among the tourist traps.
→ [Read the full review](/food/malaysia/kuala-lumpur/street-food/warung-rj/)

---

## Ngau Kee Beef Noodles
*Bukit Bintang, KL · Chinese, Noodles*
Simple late-night beef noodle spot that is perfect after drinks around Changkat — minimal menu but everything is flavourful.
→ [Read the full review](/food/malaysia/kuala-lumpur/street-food/ngau-kee-beef-noodles/)

---

## ABC One Bistro
*Brickfields, KL · Malaysian*
Busy 24-hour eatery with excellent roti canai and dips — chaotic but a solid option around Brickfields.
→ [Read the full review](/food/malaysia/kuala-lumpur/street-food/abc-one-bistro/)

---

## Nagasari Curry House
*Bukit Bintang, KL · Indian, Malay*
Very basic hole-in-the-wall serving inexpensive curries — tasty enough for a quick cheap meal around Bukit Bintang.
→ [Read the full review](/food/malaysia/kuala-lumpur/street-food/nagasari-curry-house/)

---

## Warisan Nasi Kukus
*NU Sentral, KL · Malay*
The nasi lemak lacks flavour but the side dishes, especially lamb and fried chicken, are genuinely enjoyable.
→ [Read the full review](/food/malaysia/kuala-lumpur/street-food/warisan-nasi-kukus/)
