# Restaurants

---

## Fat Cupid
*Pantai Tengah · Malay*
Beautifully designed restaurant with impressive presentations of traditional Malay dishes that retain authentic flavours — easily one of the better restaurants in Langkawi.
→ [Read the full review](/food/malaysia/langkawi/restaurants/fat-cupid/)

---

## Scarborough Fish & Chips
*Tanjung Rhu · British*
Excellent fish and chips directly on the beach with beautiful sea views — crisp batter, proper chips, great value at around RM30.
→ [Read the full review](/food/malaysia/langkawi/restaurants/scarborough-fish-and-chips/)

---

## Istanbul Restaurant
*Pantai Cenang · Turkish*
A proper upscale Turkish restaurant, not a kebab shop — excellent lamb kebab platter and hummus at prices that justify the quality.
→ [Read the full review](/food/malaysia/langkawi/restaurants/istanbul-restaurant/)

---

## Tapaz Langkawi
*Pantai Cenang · Fusion, Tapas*
Great marina-side location and really good food — chicken liver, squid salad, lamb balls — let down by shocking service and a limited wine list.
→ [Read the full review](/food/malaysia/langkawi/restaurants/tapaz-langkawi/)

---

## Janggus Cenang Seafood
*Pantai Cenang · Seafood*
Jam-packed with Malaysians and for good reason — the fresh grilled grouper with delicious sambal is excellent value at 50 RM.
→ [Read the full review](/food/malaysia/langkawi/restaurants/janggus-cenang-seafood/)

---

## Yasmin Syrian Restaurant
*Pantai Cenang · Syrian*
Very authentic Syrian and Middle Eastern food with excellent appetisers and particularly good hummus.
→ [Read the full review](/food/malaysia/langkawi/restaurants/yasmin-syrian-restaurant/)

---

## Bella Restaurant
*Pantai Cenang · Malay, International*
Simple tourist restaurant right on the Cenang strip — decent food, fair prices, perfectly serviceable but nothing memorable.
→ [Read the full review](/food/malaysia/langkawi/restaurants/bella-restaurant/)

---

## Colonial India Restaurant
*Langkawi · Indian*
Fairly good food but oddly lifeless atmosphere, service lacks warmth, portions are small, and the mango lassi is genuinely awful.
→ [Read the full review](/food/malaysia/langkawi/restaurants/colonial-india-restaurant/)

---

**Price level note:**
Price levels refer to the approximate cost for **two people** ordering an average meal **without alcoholic drinks**.
