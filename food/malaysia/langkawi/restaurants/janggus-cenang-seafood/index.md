A very inviting place jam-packed with Malaysians. I chose a grouper that had just arrived and looked very fresh, plus some squid for the BBQ and seafood nasi goreng to accompany it. Service was prompt and friendly, the fish was excellent in freshness and texture with a delicious sambal. A medium sized grouper cost 50 RM, which is quite reasonable.
