Probably the best coffee shop in Kuching. The cappuccinos are expertly prepared, the surroundings are quiet and comfortable and prices remain reasonable for the quality. The only drawback is the location well outside the city centre, but if you are anywhere near Vivacity this place is absolutely worth the detour.
