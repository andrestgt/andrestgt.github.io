# Cafés

Ipoh's Old Town has half a dozen excellent heritage-building cafés serving good espresso drinks and fusion food.

---

## Aud's Cafe
*Old Town, Ipoh · Café*
Popular Old Town café with very good coffees and creative Western-Asian fusion food including the excellent spaghetti pork buddies.
→ [Read the full review](/food/malaysia/ipoh/cafes/auds-cafe/)

---

## Burps & Giggles
*Old Town, Ipoh · Café*
The quirkiest and most attractive of the Old Town cafés — the best flat white in Ipoh and free WiFi.
→ [Read the full review](/food/malaysia/ipoh/cafes/burps-and-giggles/)

---

## Captain Fish
*Medan Ipoh Bestari, Ipoh · Café*
Excellent piccolo coffee and, without hyperbole, the best salmon croissant sandwich ever — generous salmon, poached egg, almond flakes, capers.
→ [Read the full review](/food/malaysia/ipoh/cafes/captain-fish/)
