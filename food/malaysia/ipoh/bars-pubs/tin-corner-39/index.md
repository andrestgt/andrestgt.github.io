New gastro bar right by the bridge in Ipoh's Old Town — a great location and the most happening place in the area. Their drink options are a bit limited, mostly bottled beers (RM26 for a large Guinness), and you order food at the counter. However, their food is fantastic — the mixed mini bao platter and the unagi bowl were gorgeous and good value.
