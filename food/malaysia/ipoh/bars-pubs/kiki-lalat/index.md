Friendly little Old Town craft beer bar with lots of international bottled varieties and some local ones on tap. The local Toddy is actually perfect for the climate here. They do a refreshing lemongrass beer and one of the smoothest, fruitiest black beers I've ever tried. Definitely worth a try. Sadly there's no grub available, not even simple beer snacks.
