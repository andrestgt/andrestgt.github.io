Quite simply a wonderful place and well worth a stop on any Alsace trip. The restaurant has a lovely terrace overlooking the beautiful countryside and the food is quite spectacular for a humble non-Michelin hotel restaurant. Pan-fried melt-in-your-mouth foie gras, check. Munster-filled beignets, check. Prices are mid-range and perfectly acceptable for this quality of cooking. Magnifique.
