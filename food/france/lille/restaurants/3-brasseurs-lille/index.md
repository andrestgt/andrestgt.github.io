The area around Lille Flandres station is not the most pleasant part of the city. This brewery restaurant, however, is a great find. I usually come here for their good selection of craft beers and their excellent frites whenever I'm in town. Prices are very reasonable, and they even have a happy hour with some of the cheapest pints you'll find in Lille. Recommended.
