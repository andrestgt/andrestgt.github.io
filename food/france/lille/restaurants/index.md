# Restaurants in Lille


## 3 BRASSEURS LILLE
Lille · Brewpub
A great find near the station with a good selection of craft beers and excellent frites. Very reasonable prices and a happy hour with some of the cheapest pints in Lille. Recommended.
→ [Read the full review](./3-brasseurs-lille/)

## LE SOUK
Lille · Moroccan Restaurant
Nice Moroccan-themed décor and good, authentic food with large portions. The tajines and couscous are excellent, and the Moroccan wines a nice addition.
→ [Read the full review](./le-souk/)

## TBILISI
Lille · Georgian Restaurant
A tiny Georgian restaurant that's quite hard to get into - and for good reason. Sumptuous starters, wonderfully greasy khachapuri, and delicious stews. Georgian cuisine remains one of the most underrated in the world.
→ [Read the full review](./tbilisi/)
