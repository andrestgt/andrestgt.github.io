# Bars & Pubs in Paris


## CHEZ GEORGES
Paris · Wine Bar
Unique wine cellar atmosphere. Best to order a bottle of wine, some cheese, and stay a while. A lot of tourists, but the setting is hard to beat.
→ [Read the full review](./chez-georges/)

## ONZE BAR
Paris · Bar
Wonderful little bohemian neighbourhood bar with surprisingly low prices for Paris. The charcuterie and cheese platters are genuinely excellent. Few dive-looking bars produce food this good.
→ [Read the full review](./onze-bar/)

## QUAI DE BOURBON
Paris · Wine Bar
Nice place on Île Saint-Louis for a glass of wine and some homemade dips. Considering the location, prices are actually fairly reasonable.
→ [Read the full review](./quai-de-bourbon/)
