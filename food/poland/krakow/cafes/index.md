# Cafés in Kraków


## CAMELOT CAFE
Kraków · Café
Undeniably beautiful décor, the main reason to come. Service is pretty bad and prices feel high for Kraków, though the drinks are creative.

## COFFEE KIOSK KRAKOW
Kraków · Café
A cute little hipster coffee kiosk that does exactly what it says on the tin. The cappuccino is 8 zł, though slightly milkier than ideal.

## WESOŁA CAFE
Kraków · Café
Probably the best café in town. Comfy seats, good cappuccino (10 zł), and generous sandwiches. Not cheap, obviously.
