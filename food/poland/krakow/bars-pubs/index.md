# Bars & Pubs in Kraków


## ALCHEMIA OD KUCHNI
Kraków · Pub & Bar
This place oozes atmosphere. A little pricey by local standards, but popular for a reason. The wheat beer on tap is very good.
→ [Read the full review](./alchemia-od-kuchni/)

## ESZEWERIA
Kraków · Pub
Dark, cavernous, and pleasantly moody - a proper little pub. Drinks are reasonably priced. Service can be painfully slow when busy.
→ [Read the full review](./eszeweria/)

## JAZZ ROCK CAFE
Kraków · Rock Club
There's no jazz here and it isn't a café either - it's a full-blown rock club. Busy, loud, and with a surprisingly young crowd.
→ [Read the full review](./jazz-rock-cafe/)

## PIĘKNY PIES
Kraków · Bar
Big, spacious bar/club with a very casual feel and cheap drinks. An easy place to spend an evening without much ceremony.
→ [Read the full review](./piekny-pies/)

## PIJALNIA WÓDKI I PIWA
Kraków · Bar
Exactly what the name implies: vodka and beer. Cheap and cheerful, and does the job.
→ [Read the full review](./pijalnia-wodki-i-piwa/)

## SZYNK
Kraków · Traditional Pub
Pleasant traditional beer-and-food pub with good draft beers and local comfort food. Pickled herring and blood sausage make a lot of sense here, especially with a beer.
→ [Read the full review](./szynk/)
