# Restaurants in Kraków


## PRZYSTANEK PIEROGARNIA
Kraków · Polish Restaurant
The pierogi are genuinely delicious, and the prices are great. It's a cute little shop, and yes, the hype is justified. For quality and value, this is about as good as it gets.

## RESTAURACJA SMAKOŁYKI
Kraków · Polish Restaurant
Nice modern eatery with mainly local clientele. Cheap and filling, with straightforward Polish staples. The gołąbki are a little bland, though.
→ [Read the full review](./restauracja-smokolyki/)

## WARSZTAT PO POLSKU
Kraków · Polish Restaurant
Small place with a mix of tourists and locals, serving simple Polish food. Tasty and not expensive, though portions are small by Central European standards. Good bigos and pork neck.
