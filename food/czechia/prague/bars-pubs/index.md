# Bars & Pubs in Prague


## PIVOVARSKÝ KLUB BENEDICT
Prague · Craft Beer Bar
Mind-boggling beer selection with countless craft beers on tap and bottled. The food is very good too. Much better priced than places in the Old Town centre.

## U RUDOLFINA
Prague · Pub
Feels a little more authentic than many places in Prague's Old Town. Only Pilsner Urquell on tap, for 45 Kč. Prices not cheap, but still reasonable.
→ [Read the full review](./u-rudolfina/)
