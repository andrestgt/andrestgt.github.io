Probably the nicest café in Brno. Thankfully, it avoids the usual hipster style and instead feels more like a modern take on a traditional Kaffeehaus. The coffee is excellent, the service friendly, and while it's not cheap, prices are reasonable for the location. Cappuccino costs 65 Kč. The homemade meat pâtés are also worth trying.
