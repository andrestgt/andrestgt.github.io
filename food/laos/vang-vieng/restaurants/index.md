# Restaurants

---

## Amigo's Restaurant
*Vang Vieng · Mexican*
Surprisingly good Mexican food for a backpacker town in Southeast Asia — the burritos are nicely presented and the mango salsa is excellent.
→ [Read the full review](/food/laos/vang-vieng/restaurants/amigos-restaurant/)

---

## Viman Vang Vieng German Restaurant
*Vang Vieng · German, Thai*
Unusual little restaurant doing both Thai and German home-style food rather well — the Thai curry and German meatballs with fried potatoes were both genuinely satisfying.
→ [Read the full review](/food/laos/vang-vieng/restaurants/viman-german-restaurant/)

## Nazim Indian Restaurant
*Vang Vieng · Indian*
A big disappointment compared to its older reputation — the chicken saag tasted of nothing and what was sold as lamb korma appeared to be beef.
