In a town increasingly filled with expensive cafés and tourist-oriented restaurants, this old-school noodle shop feels like a surviving relic of another era. The noodle soup itself is simple and cheap rather than extraordinary, but eating here in front of a crumbling colonial building is a wonderful experience.
